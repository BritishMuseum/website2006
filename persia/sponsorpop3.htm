Bank Melli Iran

Bank Melli Iran was established in September 1928 by Act of Parliament as Iran’s first commercial bank and has played a key role in Iran’s economic development since that era. Bank Melli Iran is Iran’s largest bank in terms of assets and would rank as the 12th largest bank in the United Kingdom on this measure.

Today Bank Melli Iran remains the country’s leading financial institution offering a broad range of Islamic, retail and wholesale banking products to millions of customers with over 3,000 domestic branches, 16 foreign offices and in excess of 41,000 employees. 40% of all domestic deposits and 35% of all commercial bank credit in Iran are handled by Bank Melli Iran.

The Bank is an important partner to leading private and public institutions in all economic sectors and facilitates international trade and investment in Iran through its global network.
