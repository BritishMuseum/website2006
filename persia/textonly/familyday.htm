The Splendours of Ancient Persia
Saturday 5 November, 11.00 – 16.30, Admission free*

Come and celebrate one of the most fascinating civilizations of the Ancient Near East.

This spectacular family day includes a host of free activities for adults and children. Watch traditional dancers, listen to stories and music and have an audience with great King Xerxes himself. There will also be a treasure hunt for the children with a chance to win prizes.

For a full programme visit www.thebritishmuseum.ac.uk/education.

*There is an admission charge for the exhibition.
