Persian Poetry & Europe's Enlightenment
Saturday 8 October, 14.00–20.00

Classical, mystic, modern. Guest speakers Homa Katouzian (Oxford University), Nima Mina (SOAS), Alan Williams (Manchester University) and Narguess Farzad (SOAS) discuss and illustrate aspects of Iran’s rich poetic tradition and its continuing importance. Followed by a concert of classical Persian music.

£20, BM Friends £18, concessions £14



Handel’s Belshazzar
Saturday 29 October, 18.30

The Enlightenment’s fascination with Persia transposed in the grand surroundings of the Nereid Gallery. Conducted by Anthony Milledge. With Canonbury Chamber Choir and Whitecross Orchestra.

£10



Music & Poetry
Friday 16 December, 18.30

A magical evening in the Museum’s historic Reading Room with classical and modern poetry read in Persian and English. Accompanied by music, and a traditional Persian spread to celebrate Yalda (winter solstice).

£5, concessions £3
