BP is delighted to support Forgotten Empire: the World of Ancient Persia , which beautifully illuminates the unique contributions of Iranian culture to world civilisation and its dedication to humankind. It is hoped this exhibition will promote renewed interest in this remarkable region of the world and admiration for its extraordinary heritage and achievements.

BP is the British Museum ’s largest and most longstanding corporate sponsor, supporting the Museum on an annual basis since 1996. Since 2003, BP has been the major exhibition sponsor at the British Museum . BP serves around 13 million customers in more than 100 countries across six continents each day – providing products that contribute to a better quality of life, providing the freedom to move, to heat, and to see.
