Past Practice

Future Prospects

12-14 September 2001

The Clore Education Centre Great Court

The British Museum



The Department of Conservation of The British Museum is holding an international conference consisting of two main themes - the history of conservation and the future practice of conservation.

The 'history' papers will explore the careers of important and influential conservators, record the history of conservation in a number of institutions around the world, or examine the history of certain conservation techniques. In the main, the period covered stops in the mid 20th century.

'Future practice' is about the challenges that the conservation profession has to face in the 21st century.
