Conservation Library
at 9 Montague Street WC1B 3DG

The Department of Conservation has an extensive library comprising works on all aspects of conservation practice. The library may be used by bona fide students of conservation attending recognised courses at academic institutions in the UK and the rest of the world. Any application for the use of the library should be made by post to The Keeper of Conservation in the first instance and should be accompanied by a brief note from a tutor to confirm the student's status. The address is: The Keeper, Department of Conservation, The British Museum, London WC1B 3DG.

Subject to the Keeper's approval, students should telephone the general office at the above address on 0207 323 8575 or email conservation@british-museum.ac.uk and arrange an appointment. We are unable to allow students access without prior appointment
