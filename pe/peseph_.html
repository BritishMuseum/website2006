How to Order

Orders for photographs of objects in the department's collections (with the exception of the horological collection) can be placed with the photographic librarian, Ms. Sovati Smith, in the following ways:

email:
ssmith@thebritishmuseum.ac.uk
phone:
+44 (0)20 7323 8626
fax:
+44 (0)20 7323 8496
post:

Department of Prehistory & Europe
The British Museum
Great Russell Street
London WC1B 3DG

Please note that requests for photographs of clocks, watches, horological tools and ephemera should be directed to the Horological Study Room.

Please also note that it takes approximately four weeks to process an order.
