Family events

Admission free, just drop in

The Big Draw

Saturday 14 October, 11.00–16.00
Great Court
An annual celebration of drawing. This day will see contemporary artists working in the exhibition as well as workshops for families and individuals across the Museum in galleries relating to the Pacific.

Click here to download the full Big Draw programme

Artist in residence: George Nuku

30 October – 11 November
11.00–13.00 and 14.00–16.00
Great Court
George Nuku is a highly regarded Maori artist who works in bone, wood, shell, polystyrene and perspex. Also a performance artist, he will be based in the Great Court demonstrating his art. Just drop in to his Maori Meeting House.

Pacific Islands Day

Saturday 4 November, 12.00–16.00
Great Court
A day of celebration of the best of Pacific art and culture including sculpture with George Nuku, storytelling, performance and craft demonstrations.
