Encounters in Polynesia: chiefs, priests & powerful objects

Monday 2 October, 13.30
Stevenson Lecture Theatre
Dr Steven Hooper

The Shark God: Encounters with myth and magic in the South Pacific

Award-winning journalist Charles Montgomery explores how myths are born in his new book, The Shark God, with spectacular photos and stories from his South Pacific expedition.

Thursday 9 November, 13.30
Stevenson Lecture Theatre
Charles Montgomery

Contemporary Polynesia performances and cultural identity

Adrienne L Kaeppler, Smithsonian Institute, Washington, looks at the importance of performance to the cultural life of contemporary Polynesians, focusing on the Pacific Festivals of Art held every four years in the Pacific Islands.

Monday 4 December, 13.30
BP Lecture Theatre
Professor Adrienne L. Kaeppler
