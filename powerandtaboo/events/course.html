Course

Patterns of the sun: Pacific fibre arts and textiles

11 Monday evenings from 2 October, 18.30–20.30
Claus Moser Room

Feathered gods, Hawaiian shirts, magical threads and colourful quilts that inspired Matisse and Gauguin are among the fibre arts and textiles of the Pacific.
Tutor: Kaori O’Connor
