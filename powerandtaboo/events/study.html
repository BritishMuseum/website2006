Study day & conference

An introduction to Power & Taboo

Saturday 30 September, 14.00–16.30
Stevenson Lecture Theatre

An afternoon with the curators of the British Museum’s Power & Taboo exhibition, introducing the history and culture of Polynesia and the ideas behind its presentation in the exhibition.

£15, Members and concessions £12

 

Pacific Islands Public Conference

Friday 1 and Saturday 2 December
Stevenson Lecture Theatre

Convened by the British Museum and the Sainsbury Research Unit for the Arts of Africa, Oceania & the Americas, University of East Anglia.

Friday 1 December: Taboo in the Pacific: managing sanctity
10.30–19.00: Talks and academic papers relating to the exhibition Power & Taboo.

Saturday 2 December: The Pacific Islands in Britain
14.00–20.00: Talks and demonstrations by Pacific Islands artists and cultural groups, followed by performances.
In partnership with the Pacific Islands Society of the UK and Ireland (PISUKI)

Full Programme


Each day: £18, concessions and members of PISUKI £12
