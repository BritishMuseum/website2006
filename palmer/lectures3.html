Stevenson Lecture Theatre, £5, concessions £3

Thursday 19 January, 18.30
The Rediscovery of Palmer:
Graham Sutherland and Kenneth Clark

Martin Hammer (Edinburgh University) traces the influence of Palmer’s vision on the Sutherland circle and how it eventually led to the purchase of his A Cornfield by Moonlight by the British Museum in 1985.
