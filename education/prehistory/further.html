Iron Age / Celtic Europe and Britain for Adults and Students
This Bibliography is not comprehensive, but is intended to provide enough titles for teachers and students to study the Museum collections in The Celtic Europe Gallery (Room 50). The study of the Iron Age is full of controversy and ideas in modern works do not always receive universal agreement.
