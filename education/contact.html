Enquiries
The Learning and Information Department will try and assist with your enquiry. However, it is not always possible to provide a speedy reply as some enquiries involve other departments and complex answers.

Please try and remember to include the following when contacting the Learning and Information Department by email:

Your name
Your postal address
An enquiry without an address delays the information reaching you.
Fax or telephone no.
