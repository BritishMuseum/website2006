   Call for Papers
Chinese Arts in the International Arena
18th - 20th April 2002

A collaboration between the Chinese Arts Centre, The British Museum and the Centre for Art International Research (cair), Liverpool John Moores University.

Outline proposals should be written in English and of a maximum of 750 words. In addition, please provide relevant biographical information (maximum of 250 words) including recent research, publications, exhibitions and events, and your current working role.

Selected speakers may be invited to contribute an essay based on their paper for inclusion in a book, based on issues arising from the conference.
