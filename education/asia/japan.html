Recommended Reading:
  • L. Smith, V. Harris, T. Clark, Japanese Art: Masterpieces from the British Museum (BMP, 1990)
    This book examines the extensive collection of Japanese art and antiquities in the British Museum. Material ranges from prehistoric archaeology to twentieth century prints, and includes sculpture, metalwork, painting and ceramics. The text relates them to each other and to the wider culture of East Asia.
