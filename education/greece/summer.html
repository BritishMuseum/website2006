Ancient Greece Family Day

11 July

Thousands of people turned up to our last Family Day where they
  • Met some ancient Greek warriors, tried on helmets and found out what it feels like to carry a shield.
     
  • Listened to the story of the Trojan War - Helen, Paris, the golden apple, Hector and mighty Achilles.
     
  • Visited Greece through the Ages with our fantastic new Hamlyn Family Trail.
     
  • Saw Kirk Douglas as Ulysses as he battled the fearsome one-eyed giant Cyclops.
     
  • The highlight of the day was the great procession to honour the goddess Athena. Children decorated Greek clothes, helped build an Athenian ship and made gifts for the goddess. Finally hundreds marched in a great procession to celebrate her birthday.
