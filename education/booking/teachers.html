News

You can now book tickets for many British Museum events online.

Admission ticket to the British Museum London, England, 3 March AD 1790
  

For single date events such as study days and events for teachers, you can use the booking form, facsimile, post or telephone. Such events carry a four letter event code after their title. For all other events, please book by telephone. The Box Office is open Monday to Friday 10:00-17:30 including half-terms and school holidays: telephone 020 7323 8181. You are also welcome to book in person.

Cheques should be made payable to 'The British Museum Great Court Ltd'.

We will confirm all bookings for visits and events by post. You must bring your confirmation forms with you when you arrive for your visit or event. If you do not receive a confirmation form within two weeks of booking, please contact us immediately.

Cancellations
If you cancel a booking less than 14 days before an event for schools, we will try to substitute from our waiting list. If this is not possible, your fee will not be refunded.

In the case of courses for teachers, we are not able to refund fees for cancellations received less than 14 days before the course. If a course has to be cancelled, we will inform you not less than 14 days before the date of the course. In the event of a cancellation, we will refund the full fee, but cannot take responsibility for travel or other costs you may incur.
