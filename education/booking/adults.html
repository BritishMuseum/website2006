You can now book tickets for many British Museum events online.

Admission ticket to the British Museum London, England, 3 March AD 1790
   All bookings for adult events, unless otherwise stated, can be made by calling the Box Office on 020 7323 8181 10:00-17:30 daily. Alternatively fill out the booking form and send a cheque payable to 'The British Museum Great Court Ltd' to:

Box Office
The British Museum
Great Russell Street
London
WC1B 3DG

or facsimile 020 7323 8110 (credit/debit cards only). Selected events can be booked online.

We cannot offer refunds unless in exceptional circumstances. We reserve the right to cancel a course or event, with refund of ticket price, if it does not reach minimum numbers.

Concessionary rates apply to senior citizens, students on state funded courses and registered unemployed. Proof of status may be required on the day of the event. If booking by post, a photocopy showing current status should be enclosed.

If you have special access requirements please inform the Box Office or the Learning and Information Department in advance.

For further information on any of the adult events, please contact the Learning and Information Department.
