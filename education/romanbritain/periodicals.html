Featured Book

Roman Britain
This is an authoritative account of Roman Britain, written by two leading scholars in the field. The late Tim Potter was Keeper of Prehistory and Early Europe in The British Museum and Catherine Johns has been a curator for Roman Britain for 34 years. Catherine has updated the book to include recent material like the Hoxne hoard.

T. W. Potter and C. Johns, The British Museum Press 2002.
ISBN: 0 7141 2774 4
