AZTECS & MEXICO

  • Bateman, P. Aztecs and Incas. Great Civilizations. Aladdin Books Ltd, 1988
    A general introduction for children.
  • Benson A. & Sayer G. & C. Of Gods and Men. BBC Publications, 1980
    The relationship between pre-Hispanic and modern Mexican cultures described clearly and simply.
  • Carmichael E. & Sayer, C. The Skeleton at the Feast. The Day of the Dead in Mexico. BMP, 1991
    An overall introduction to the festival with a series of interviews with artists and participants in the festival. Detailed bibliography.
  • Cortés, Ruiz Erain Los Días De Muertos. Una costumbre mexicana.
    GV editores, 1990
    In Spanish. Well-illustrated good introduction to the festival, describing different customs in different regions.
  • Lane, S. & Turkovich, M. Días de los Muertos / Days of the Dead. Associates in Multicultural and International Education, 1987
    A good introduction to the Day of the Dead, especially for secondary school students, with suggested activities and exercises. Particularly useful for ways of looking at 'calaveras' (satirical verses) and Posada prints.
  • Nicholson, R. & Watts C. The Aztecs. Two Can, 1991
    Basic introduction with photos and colourful drawings. In addition a glossary and an Aztec story.
  • Odijk, P. The Aztecs. Macmillan, Ancient World series, 1989
    A lot of information suitable for older primary children and project work with maps, timecharts and photographs.
  • Salinas-Norman, B. Folk Art Traditions II. Pinata Publications, 1988
    In both English and Spanish. Excellent book of crafts, activities and ideas based on the Day of the Dead festival, including altar decorations, toys, food, theatre and dance.
  • Sayer, C. Arts and Crafts of Mexico. Thames and Hudson, 1990
    Good introduction and beautifully illustrated.
  • Sayer, C. Mexico: The Day of the Dead. An Anthology. Redstone Press, 1990
    A box of delights with small illustrated book, tin skeleton, papercut, prayer-sheet and Posada print, and detail of Diego Rivera's mural Day of the Dead (City Fiesta).
  • Wood, M. Ancient America. Cultural Atlas for Young People. Equinox, 1990
    A clear summary of the ancient history of North, Central and South America, with good colour photographs, maps and drawings.
