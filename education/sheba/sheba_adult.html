Handling Workshop - Yemeni Culture
Saturday 7 September, 11.00-13.00 Clore Education Centre
Cost: £10, BM Friends £8, concessions £6
This workshop offers an excellent opportunity to examine closely the ceramics, textiles and basketry of contemporary Yemen, and to explore their cultural and creative contexts.
Tutor: Shelagh Weir
Tickets available from the British Museum Box Office on 020 7323 8181

 

Seminar for Arabian Studies

Thursday 18 - Saturday 20 July, BP Lecture Theatre, Clore Education Centre
Cost: £25 per day or £70 for whole conference, concessions (BM Friends and students) £8 per day or £20 for whole conference.
An annual academic conference on all aspects of pre-modern Arabia. This year many papers will focus on the archaeology and history of pre-Islamic and Islamic Yemen. The end of the conference will be marked by a concert of Yemeni music on the evening of Saturday 20 July.

For further details please contact Venetia Porter in the Department of Oriental Antiquities by letter or fax 020 7323 8561.
