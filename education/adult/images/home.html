Adult Learners Week 2004
15-21 May

Join in this nationwide celebration at the British Museum. A daily programme of performances, object handling, workshops and much more. Contact the Education Department for a leaflet.

© Photo: Charles Shearn

 

We offer a range of events for the adult general public who are interested in deepening their understanding of an area of the collections or the subject of an exhibition.

EyeOpener gallery tours
These are given by specially trained volunteer guides. There are eight daily introductory tours to a wide range of galleries, plus spotlights. Free, no booking required.

Gallery talks
These are given by Museum staff and visiting lecturers; they focus on specific topics. Free, no booking required, but they may be taking place in a charging exhibition.

Study days
These allow more intensive exploration of the cultural background to an exhibition or area of the collections; they usually include slide lectures and gallery talks.

Events leaflet
BMF members receive it free. To subscribe, join the mailing list: £3.00 a year (UK and Overseas Surface Mail). Send cheques payable to 'The British Museum' to Mailing List, The British Museum, London WC1B 3DG.
