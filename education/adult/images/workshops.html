Drawing objects from the Pacific

Meet in the studio, Clore Education Centre.
Thursday 22 January,10.30 - 13.30

Use the strange and wonderful objects and the splendid surroundings of the new Enlightenment Gallery to develop your drawing techniques. The emphasis is on objects and art from the Pacific including contemporary works by the artist Rebecca Jewell on display in the Clore Education Centre. All levels welcome. Materials supplied.

Tutor: Rebecca Jewell
£15, BM Friends & concessions £12
12 places

Practical printmaking: Engraving

Friday 6 February, 14.00 - 17.00
Clore Education Centre

Toni Martina, artist and tutor, demonstrates how artists like Dürer made prints in the different intaglio techniques of copper plate, engraving and drypoint. This workshop concentrates on engraving and you will create an original print using this method.

Supported by Dasha Shenkman
£15, BM Friends & concessions £12
12 places

Drawing in the Wellcome Trust Gallery

Monday 9 February, 10.30 - 13.30
Clore Education Centre

Be inspired by the wealth of objects in this new ethnography gallery. Ranging from the monumental to the everyday these works of art from Asia, the Americas and the Pacific all relate to health and well- being. This session includes working in the studio. Meet in the studio, Clore Education Centre.

All levels welcome
