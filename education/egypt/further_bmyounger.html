Learning and Information Department > Egypt > Further Reading > BMP publications: for younger readers
 

Below is a selection of the books published by the British Museum Press on Egypt. All the books can be purchased online by following the links, or in the Museum bookshop. They can also be accessed free of charge in the Paul Hamlyn Library in the Reading Room at the heart of the Museum.

Books for younger readers

Donoughue, Carol The mystery of the hieroglyphs 1999 1995


Filer, Joyce The mystery of the Egyptian mummy 2003

Harris, Geraldine & Pemberton, Delia The British Museum illustrated encyclopaedia of ancient Egypt 1999 1995

Hart, George Pocket dictionary of ancient Egyptian gods and goddesses 2001

Mcdonald, Angela The British Museum pocket dictionary of ancient Egyptian animals 2004

Morris, Neil Everyday life in ancient Egypt 2003

Parkinson, Richard Pocket guide to Ancient Egyptian hieroglyphs 2003

Pemberton, Delia Egyptian mummies: people from the past 2000

All books published by The British Museum Press, London
