Below is a selection of the books published by the British Museum Press on Egypt. All the books can be purchased online by following the links, or in the Museum bookshop. They can also be accessed free of charge in the Paul Hamlyn Library in the Reading Room at the heart of the Museum.

Adults/general books

Andrews, Carol Egyptian mummies 2nd edition, 1998

Andrews, Carol The Rosetta stone 1981

Davies, W.V. & Friedmann, R. Egypt 1998


Davies, W.V. Egyptian hieroglyphs 1987


Hart, George Egyptian myths 1990


Nicholson, P. & Shaw, Ian The British Museum dictionary of Ancient Egypt 1995


Stafford-Deitsch, J. The monuments of Ancient Egypt 1995


Tiradritti, F, Ancient Egypt: art, architecture and history 2002


All books published by The British Museum Press, London
