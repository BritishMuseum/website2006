Schools: Gallery sessions(KS2)

Explorer Pack Sessions

Museum Explorer Packs provide you with everything you need for a lively and challenging teaching session in the Museum galleries, including objects, samples of materials, photographs and a range of activity cards. You can use a Pack on its own, combine it with your own resources or adapt it to the specific needs of your pupils. After completing an after-school training session, you will be able to use the Museum Explorer Pack free of charge with your class.

Materials of ancient Egypt

This pack is specially designed to link with KS2 Science: Materials and their properties and will enable pupils to handle materials that would have been used in ancient Egypt.

Symbols of the pharaoh

The activities in this pack focus more on the history and art of ancient Egypt. They encourage pupils to look more closely at Egyptian sculptures and equip them to begin to decipher the meanings they had for the ancient viewer.

Please see the 2005/6 edition of the Information and events for schools and teachers brochure for further details.
