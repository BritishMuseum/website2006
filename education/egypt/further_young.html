The books below have been selected for younger readers interested in learning more about the history of Egypt.

Books for younger readers

Allen, K. One Day in Tutankhamun's Egypt. Tyndall, 1973

Boase, W. A Closer Look at Ancient Egypt. Scimitar, 1981

Caselli, G. An Egyptian Craftsman. Macdonald, 1986

David, R. & A. E. History as Evidence: Ancient Egypt. Kingfisher Books, 1985

Fay, B. The Golden Serpent. Philipp von Zabern, 1989

Harrison, S. & P. Egypt. Landmarks Series, BBC Educational Publishing, 1990

Hart, G. Exploring the Past: Ancient Egypt. Pyramid Books, 1988

Hart, G. Eyewitness Guides: Ancient Egypt. Dorling Kindersley, 1990

Millard, A. The Egyptians. Macdonald Educational, 1981

Millard, A. Great Civilisations: Ancient Egypt. Longman, 1983

Saunders, B. British Museum Paper Pageants: Egyptians. Jonathan Cape and British Museum Press, 1988

Unstead, R. J. See inside and Egyptian Town. Hutchinson, 1977
