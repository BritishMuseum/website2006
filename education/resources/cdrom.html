Journeys in the Roman Empire
An interactive exploration of Britain and the Roman Empire

Sam Moorhead published in association with Channel 4 2001, CD ROM for PC/MAC

CD Romulus is your guide on any of four journeys through the Empire: you could accompany a Roman merchant, the Emperor Hadrian, the Palmyrene Barates, or the Christian saint Melania.

Illustrated with a rich array of artefacts from all over the Roman Empire from the collections of the British Museum and Verulamium Museum in St. Albans, and with a wide variety of scene setting images and graphics, the archaeology and history of the Roman Empire are presented in an authoritative yet lively and engaging manner.

With integrated teachers' support materials.
