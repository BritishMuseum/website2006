Learning pages : Schools

Mummy: the inside story offers a great opportunity for schools to support Egypt and Science in the classroom. One teacher who brought a class to a preview reported: 'it was the culmination of our project on Ancient Egypt, reinforcing what they had found out at school - and a fun morning out'.

Special sessions for schools

From 13 September 2004 - 16 March 2005 on Mondays, Tuesdays and Wednesdays during term time there will be special education sessions at 10.30 and 11.20 led by trained facilitators. These are intended primarily for KS2 but can also be adapted for other age groups. The session lasts for 45 minutes; maximum group size 109; £3 per person

For schools bookings please contact the Box Office on 020 7323 8181.
