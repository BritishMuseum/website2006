Learning pages : Access

Every effort has been made to make Mummy: the inside story accessible to as many visitors as possible, including those with a range of disabilities.

For bookings please contact the Box Office on 020 7323 8181 and explain your particular requirements.


Please choose one of the following:

Hearing impaired visitors Visually impaired visitors Physical access

Visually impaired visitors

All the written information in the exhibition is in large print
The gallery assistants have been trained to guide visually impaired visitors.
Replicas of objects reflecting those highlighted in the film and in the display cases will be available to visually impaired visitors from the gallery assistants in the last room of the exhibition.

A booklet has been produced in large print, with the text of the film and all the exhibition labels. This is available from the Information Desk in the Great Court and both Box Offices.

Flyer

A flyer introducing the exhibition, giving information about the visual elements of the film and locating what is in each room has been produced in Braille, large print and audio CD. Available in all forms at the Information Desk in the Great Court and at both box offices.

Physical access
For wheelchair users

  • There is lift access to the exhibition
  • There is a permanent wheelchair space available in the auditorium
  • All the displays are at a suitable height

For visitors with mobility problems
There are a limited number of seats in the first room and last room, so please make yourself known to the gallery assistants who will try to ensure that you are able to sit comfortably
