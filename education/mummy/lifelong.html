Mummy: the inside story

Learning pages : Lifelong learners

Mummy: the inside story offers fascinating insights, not only for those studying ancient Egypt, but for many other subjects including forensic science, the ethics of conservation, and public applications of modern technology.
It is also of huge general interest – no previous knowledge of Egypt or of Science is assumed.
