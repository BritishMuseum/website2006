Featured Book

Thai Textiles
Thailand is a country with a rich cultural heritage especially evident in its woven costumes and textiles. This fascinating book fully explores the role textiles play in the lives of the Thai people.

Susan Conway, The British Museum Press 1992.
ISBN: 0 7141 2506 7
