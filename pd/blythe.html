Blythe House, 23 Blythe Road, London W14 0QF

Access for visitors to the Prints and Drawings collection

Because of lack of space at Bloomsbury, the engraved portraits and certain other parts of the collection of the Department of Prints and Drawings have been moved to a store at Blythe House. The storage area is normally open by appointments on Tuesday from 10.00 to 13.00, but because visitors have to be escorted from the Control Room to the storage area, all appointments should be made for 10.00.

Appointments should be made with the Students' Room staff at Bloomsbury on +44 (0)20 7323 8408, and names and telephone numbers of visitors are kept in the appointment book on the Superintendent's Desk. Appointments must be made by Monday morning so that a list of visitors can be faxed to Blythe House on Monday afternoon. Security staff at Blythe House will have a copy of this list and visitors must give their names at the electronically-operated gate before being allowed through.
