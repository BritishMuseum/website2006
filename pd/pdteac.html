teaching          
 

As well as contributing to the Museum's programme of public lectures and gallery talks, staff of the Department of Prints and Drawings teach on an MA course in the History of the Print run jointly with University College London. In addition, the Michael Bromberg Fellowship offers practical training in the understanding of printmaking techniques and knowledge of print history.
