Due to the size and high quality of the collection, the Department of Prints and Drawings receive numerous requests for loans every year. At present we are lending over one thousand works a year to between seventy and eighty museums and galleries. Loans can consist of a single work, or an entire exhibition.

In collaboration with Hayward Gallery Touring we have run a programme since 1993 of entire exhibitions of prints from the collection which have been circulated to many regional galleries. Rembrandt the Printmaker is touring to the following venues:

Hull, Ferens Art Gallery
8 April to 18 June 2006

Bath, Victoria Art Gallery
24 June to 3 September 2006

Newcastle-upon-Tyne, Laing Art Gallery
14 September to 31 December 2006

Stoke-on-Trent, The Potteries Museum and Art Gallery
28 April to 24 June 2007

Blackpool, Grundy Art Gallery
30 June to 8 September 2007

For those planning exhibitions a sheet of advice for potential borrowers is available from the Department.
