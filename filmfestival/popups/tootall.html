Johnny Tootall

Directed by Shirley Cheechoo, 2005, 93 mins
In her second dramatic feature, set on the west coast of Vancouver Island, international award-winning filmmaker Shirley Cheechoo explores rebirth, recovery and the search for truth. Johnny (Adam Beach) journeys home following a haunting tour of duty in Bosnia. Upon his return he finds his estranged brother (Nathaniel Arcand) leading a protest to save their land. Brothers and worlds collide as Johnny grapples with the demons of his past until a tragic incident leads him to embrace his destiny.
