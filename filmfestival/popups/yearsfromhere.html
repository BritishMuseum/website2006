Years From Here: Part 2 of Changing Ground

Directed by John Walker, 2000, 50 mins
The return visit: a group from Papua New Guinea visit the villages and lands of the Sto:lo in the Fraser Valley. The Sto:lo hosts welcome their exotic visitors with drums, show them the salmon fishery and other elements of Sto:lo culture – with the poignancy of much sense of loss, especially of language. Made for TV, this is a strong and accessible journey into some of the deepest puzzles about modern Aboriginal society.
