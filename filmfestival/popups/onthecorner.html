On The Corner

Directed by Nathaniel Geary, 2003, 95 mins
This harrowing and brilliant film explores the world of those who come from First Nations to live in the city. This is a world of drugs, drifting, solitude and deep vulnerability. It is also a world where intimacy of family and of love create both acute risk and forms of redemption. On The Corner comes from Aboriginal filmmakers – director and actors are working with deep personal understanding. This is an important, powerful and disturbing film
