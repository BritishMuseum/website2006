Featuring Myths, Scripting Reality
The Second Native North American Film Festival
26-28 November 2004
Clore Education Centre
Admission free by ticket only

Featuring Myths, Scripting Reality - The Second Native North American Film Festival, held at the British Museum, 26-28 November 2004, presents nine historical and contemporary documentaries and features, by and about Native peoples of North America.

Focusing on North American peoples of the Arctic, Subarctic, and Plains, the films and videos address a wide range of themes. These include environmental and land issues, story-telling, elders and young people, cities and reservations. Questions of universal concern are explored as well as the particular histories and cultures of Native people of North America.

Native and non-native film-makers, community members and scholars will be present to introduce and discuss the films, and to answer questions from the audience.
