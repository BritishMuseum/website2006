Through These Eyes


Weaving together archival and contemporary footage, this documentary revisits the making and reception of the Netsilik Film Series in Cold War America. Interviews with some of the Inuit participants illuminate the changes that have taken place in Pelly Bay in the last four decades.
(Dir.: Charles Laird. 2004. 54 min).

Introduction by the Honourable Peter Irniq (Commissioner of Nunavaut) and Asen Balikci (ethnographer and filmmaker)

Panel discussion with the Honourable Peter Irniq (Commissioner of Nunavaut), Asen Balikci (ethnographer and filmmaker), Hugh Brody (filmmaker and anthropologist), and Zebedee Nungak (writer and journalist from Kangirsuk, Nunavik)
