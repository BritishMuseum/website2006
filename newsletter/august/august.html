Wear it! Make it! Play it! Hear it!

Daily in August, 11.00–16.00
Join us in the Great Court for an entertaining summer programme of activities based around the many cultures of Africa, with things to make, headgear to try on, musical instruments to listen to and games to play.

Parent & child Africa trails
Free 7-days-a-week, just ask in the Reading Room

3-5 years? Chasing Rainbows with Mama Wata
Pick up colours and shapes as you follow Mama Wata’s trail through the different countries of Africa – then get creative at home.

5-11 years? The African Adventure Backpack
Activity based tour of the Sainsbury African Galleries. Make a mask, spot animal antics and recognise African pots with a blindfold on.

Other trails and backpacks include
Hunting for Dragons, Dancing with Shiva, Sailing on the Nile, Ancient Egypt and Ancient Greece

Africa Garden

Until 4 September, 9.00–17.30

Discover what links African plants and the cultures they come from in Charlie Dimmock’s last Ground Force creation – the Africa Garden at the British Museum. The Garden includes special sculptures by artists from countries like Mozambique and Ghana.

Why not choose your favourite plant or sculpture and then draw a picture with free materials from the Museum’s Reading Room?

Admission free
