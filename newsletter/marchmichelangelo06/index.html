Michelangelo, Ideal head of a woman, c. 1525-8

An outstanding opportunity to follow the evolution of some of the world’s most celebrated artworks.

This exhibition reunites material not seen together since the dispersal of Michelangelo's studio more than 400 years ago, offering a wholly different perspective on the defining genius of the Italian Renaissance.

Michelangelo,
A male nude, c.1504-5
The Teyler Museum, Haarlem

This exhibition audio guide gives fascinating insight into the stories behind the drawings and includes extracts from an interview with Hugo Chapman, curator of this exhibition.

The audio guides are lightweight, compact MP3 devices, which can be worn around the neck or over the shoulder comfortably.

There are descriptive introductions for visually impaired visitors, and the guides are available in English, French, Spanish and Italian.
