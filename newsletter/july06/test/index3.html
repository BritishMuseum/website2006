WELCOME

To celebrate the last month of the Michelangelo Drawings exhibition, the Museum will be running Michelangelo ‘til Midnight, giving you the chance to get closer to the master until midnight every Saturday in June. Complete your evening by choosing from a delicious selection of Italian antipasti, fine desserts, wine, beer and coffee, all on sale in the Great Court from 6pm.

There are also still a vast array of ways to engage more deeply with the fascinating culture of the Middle East, through the variety of films, lectures, music and performance that complement the exhibition Word into Art: Artists of the Modern Middle East

The Guardian/British Museum Public Forum
Religion, politics, sexuality
What can history tell us about the boundaries of expression in the Middle East now? Chaired by Jon Snow

Tuesday 13 June, refreshments and private view from 19.00, debate 20.00-21.30, £10, concessions £8

Religion, politics and sexuality are contested subjects within artistic expression and also raise fundamental questions about boundaries and taboos in art. A panel of international speakers look at the history and contemporary practice of artists of the Middle East and debate these issues.
