The Warren Cup (detail)

 Warren Cup: sexuality in the
ancient world
11 May – 2 July 2006

The Warren Cup is a Roman silver
drinking cup dating to the 1st Century
AD. Decorated with sexual scenes it
shows two pairs of male lovers. This
small exhibition offers a close focus on
this drinking vessel and its cultural
context.

Admission free, Room 3
