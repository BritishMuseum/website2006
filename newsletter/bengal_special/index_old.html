WELCOME

Explore the rich cultural heritage of Bengal in the British Museum's Voices of Bengal season. The season of events, lectures, displays, films and performances has something for everyone.

Find out more about Bengal and its unique cultural landscape in our series of lectures, or explore the life and work of Rabindranath Tagore through the Tagore study day or Tagore on film.

The Bengali film season is packed with classics from the region, including the Apu Trilogy and the Hindi classic Devdas – see the full list of films here. Families can enjoy free storytelling, craft demonstrations and performances throughout the season. During the October half term (23–27 October) Enthralling Bengal offers families a range of free family events. And don't miss the Bengali community family day, Bengali Blast!
