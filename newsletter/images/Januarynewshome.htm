Happy New Year from the British Museum

2003 will be a special year for the British Museum as we are celebrating our 250th Anniversary! For a quarter of a millennium the British Museum has been visited by people from all over the world curious about the legacies of the great civilisations. Throughout the anniversary year there will be many exciting events and exhibitions - stay tuned with our regular newsletter.
