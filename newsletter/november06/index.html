WELCOME
Journey over the world's
greatest archaeological sites in
November when you visit
The Past from Above:
Through the lens of
Georg Gerster. Gerster's
stunning aerial photographs
will profile over 100
archaeological and heritage
sites, compiled from 40 years
of work. Click here to book
tickets for the exhibition
now. Events to mark the
opening include a lecture by Dr
Donny George, formerly
President of the State Board of
Antiquities and Heritage in
Iraq.

Music events and films are
also on offer this month as part
of the continuing Voices of
Bengal season and Power &
Taboo exhibition.

Other highlights include the
opportunity to meet artist
Avigdor Arikha, a free
demonstration of the Japanese
tea ceremony in the renewed
Japanese Galleries, and author
Frederic Raphael discussing
his book Some talk of
Alexander. Don't miss the
Pacific Islands day on 4
November, celebrating the best
of Pacific Islands art and
culture and featuring
demonstrations by Maori artist
George Nuku in the Great
Court.
