Highlights tours

Our expert guides bring back life into the masterpieces of the world's ancient civilisations. You will not only learn about the significance of the Rosetta stone and the beauty of the Parthenon sculptures, but also explore other objects that have been carefully selected by your guide. The tour might include the Mausoleum at Halikarnassos - one of the seven wonders of the ancient world -, 'Ginger', the Sutton Hoo ship burial and many more 'must see' pieces (tours vary according to guide). The groups are limited to a maximum of 25 people with 10 people per booking and are provided in association with Tour Guides Ltd by London Tourist Board registered guides.

...and remember you can enjoy a 10% discount in the Gallery Cafes and the Court Restaurant on the day of the tour.

If you are interested in tours for groups of more than 10 people and foreign language tours ring 020 7323 8181 or email groups@thebritishmuseum.ac.uk.

EyeOpener
programme 		The eyeOpener programme offers free introductory tours throughout the Museum. Over 100 volunteer guides, trained by the British Museum Education Department, provide ten different tours a day. These tours are a great way to get to know the collections. Choose from: Ancient Egypt, The Classical world, Prehistoric/ Roman Britain, The World of Asia, Treasures of the Islamic World, The Americas, Europe: Medieval/ Modern, The Ancient Near East, The Arts & Cultures of Africa..
 
Duration Approximately 50 minutes
Charge free
Available from see timetable/ schedule

Enlightenment Gallery eyeOpener - every day at 12:30 at the meeting point in the Enlightenment gallery.

Drop in at lunchtime or on a thursday Late View and you can join an eyeOpener Spotlight talk. These take place daily at 13.15, and on Thursdays 18.15 and 19.15. Spotlights are 20 minute talks that focus on one particular aspect of the Museum and its collection. A wide variety of topics are covered, from Greek drinking parties to the Silk Route.
