The British Museum Friends (BMF) is a registered charity, a company limited by guarantee and is governed by by its Memorandum and Articles of Association. The organisation exists to support and assist the British Museum in maintaining and expanding its collections and services to scholars and the general public. In accordance with this objective BMF makes grants to the British Museum from its operating surplus and on special occasions engages in one-off fundraising campaigns to support a particular objective of the Museum.
