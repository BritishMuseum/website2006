This Guarantee is offered by all Banks and Building Societies that take part in the Direct Debit Scheme.
The efficiency and security of the Scheme is monitored and protected by your own Bank or Building Society.

If the amounts to be paid or the payment dates change BMF will notify you at least 10 days in advance of your account being debited or as otherwise agreed and if an error is made by BMF or your Bank or Building Society, you are guaranteed a full and immediate refund from your bank of the amount paid.

You can cancel a Direct Debit at any time by writing to your Bank or Building Society. Please also send a copy of your letter to us.
