As a Member you will receive three copies of The British Museum Magazine a year. The British Museum Magazine gives extensive coverage of the Museum's exhibitions and reports on its many activities such as excavations, research and publications. It provides an opportunity for curators to share their expertise and enthusiasm, and sets their work in the context of the Museum's global activities.
