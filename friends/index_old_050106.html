Would you like to support one of the world’s most prestigious and important cultural institutions? Become a member and you can enjoy a range of exciting and unique benefits, whilst also providing invaluable support for the expansion and upkeep of the British Museum’s magnificent collections.

As a member you can enjoy the following benefits:

  • A membership card that gives you unlimited free entry to all our paying exhibitions, and allows free entry for a guest every time you visit.
  • The British Museum Magazine delivered direct to your door three times a year, featuring articles about current exhibitions and the permanent collections.
  • What’s On, the Museum’s bi-monthly guide to everything that’s happening.
  • Four Member Open Evenings a year, including lectures, gallery talks, music and more.
  • Access to the Member’s sitting room overlooking the Museum’s grounds.
 


Why not give membership as a gift to a friend or family member? Membership is a great way to introduce others to the British Museum, and is an excellent gift for all occasions that can be enjoyed throughout the year.



Individual membership entitles you to your own membership card. By showing this card in the Museum you are entitled to the following benefits:
  • Free entry for you and a guest to all of the British Museum’s paying exhibitions.
  • Free entry for you and a guest to four Member Open Evenings a year, events run exclusively for members.
  • Access to the Member’s Sitting Room, situated in the west residence and overlooking the Museum’s grounds.
  • Free use of the Museum cloakroom, 10% off at all British Museum shops, cafes and restaurants courtesy of the British Museum Company, and concessions on selected Museum events and lectures (all discounts are at the discretion of the British Museum and The British Museum Company).

 

In addition you will also receive the British Museum Magazine three times annually, and the Museum’s What’s On guide six times a year.

Please print out the PDF Application form and send it to:

The British Museum Friends, The British Museum, Great Russell Street, London, WC1B 3DG.

Double membership entitles you to two separate membership cards.
This allows you the following benefits:
  • Free entry for two members and up to two guests to all of the British Museum’s paying exhibitions.
  • Free entry for two members and up to two guests to four Member Open Evenings a year, events run exclusively for members.
  • Access to the Member’s Sitting Room, situated in the west residence of the Museum and overlooking the Museum’s grounds.

 

  • Free use of the Museum cloakroom, 10% off at all British Museum shops, cafes and restaurants courtesy of the British Museum Company, and concessions on selected Museum events and lectures (all discounts are at the discretion of the British Museum and The British Museum Company).

 

In addition you will also receive the British Museum Magazine three times annually, and the Museum’s What’s On guide six times a year.

The British Museum Friends, The British Museum, Great Russell Street, London, WC1B 3DG.

Family membership entitles you to one membership card and up to three Young Friends membership cards. This entitles you to the following benefits:
  • Free entry to all of the British Museum’s paying exhibitions.
  • Free entry to four Member Evening Openings a year, events run exclusively for members.
  • Access to the Member’s Sitting Room, situated in the west residence and overlooking the Museum’s grounds.
  • Free use of the Museum cloakroom, 10% off at all British Museum shops, cafes and restaurants courtesy of the British Museum Company, and concessions on selected Museum events and lectures (all discounts are at the discretion of the British Museum and The British Museum Company).

In addition, three Young Friends receive the following:
  • ReMus, the magazine for the Young Friends of the British Museum, delivered three times a year, containing information on current Museum exhibitions and all upcoming Young Friends activities, including sleepovers.
  • Free entry to four Special Family evenings, running in conjunction with the members Evening Openings, featuring talks, craft activities, storytelling and much more.

 

Family members will also get The British Museum Magazine delivered three times annually, and the Museum’s What’s On guide six times a year.

Please print out the PDF Application form and send it to:

The British Museum Friends, The British Museum, Great Russell Street, London, WC1B 3DG.
