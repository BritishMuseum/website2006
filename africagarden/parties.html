Africa Garden Talk: Plants with a purpose
Thursday 25 August, 11.15

We will be talking about as many of the useful aspects of the plants in the 'Africa Garden' as we can fit in during the time allotted to this 'Garden Talk'..! These aspects will include wood, fibres, food, oils, resins, gums and many more. As we are both scientists we will also draw on our own analytical research to add an extra dimension to the botanical details. Having said that, our talk will be aimed at all ages and we are certainly planning to have something in it for everyone!

Caroline Cartwright and Rebecca Stacey
