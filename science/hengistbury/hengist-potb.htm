Pot B

The fabric of pot B contains the minerals amphibole and plagioclase, which are found in the metamorphic rock amphibolite. Amphibolite rocks are not found around Hengistbury Head. The nearest amphibolites occur in northern Brittany in France. This means that this pot was probably made in France.
