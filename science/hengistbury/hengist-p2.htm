The Pottery

The pottery includes distinctive high quality, wheel-thrown, cordoned wares. The potshave smooth, glossy, burnished surfaces, which vary in colour from dark grey to dark brown.They are quite different from the pottery most commonly found on Iron Age sites in Southern Britain.Some of the excavated pot sherds were analysed under a microscope at The British Museum using thin section petrography. This allows the pottery to be provenanced to find out where it was made.
