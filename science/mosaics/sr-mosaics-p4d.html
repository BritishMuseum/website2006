Turquoise Mosaics

Many different materials were used on the mosaics. On this animal head the eyes are made from yellow-banded conch shell and pyrite, surrounded by dark green malachite (a copper carbonate mineral); tiny seed pearls were used for the 'eyebrows'; the mouth is coloured with garnets and lined with small, very sharp sharks' teeth. The animal head was made from a gourd but most of the mosaics were formed on models made of wood, like the next example.
