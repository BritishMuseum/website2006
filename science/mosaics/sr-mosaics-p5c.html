Turquoise Mosaics

The tesserae were stuck onto the wood using resin, as shown in this "edge-on" view down the microscope. Analysis of the resin by GC/MS shows that it came from a pine tree.

 

In the magnified image shown here we can see that each tessera was individually polished, and shaped to fit against the tesserae next to it. The tesserae are only a millimetre or two across, and less than a millimetre thick
