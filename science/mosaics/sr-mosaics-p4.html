Turquoise Mosaics

Many different materials were used on the mosaics. On this animal head, which has been modified at some time, the eyes are made from yellow-banded conch shell and pyrite, surrounded by dark green malachite (a copper carbonate mineral); tiny seed pearls set in beeswax were used for the eyebrows; the mouth is coloured with garnets and lined with small, very sharp sharks' teeth.
