The Study of Ancient Animal Bone?

Staff at the British Museum have been experimenting with the use of radiography to show details of the structure inside ancient fish bone. This is often better preserved than the exterior and can be used for comparison with the same features in modern material. These radiographs of fish vertebrae from RA's al-Hadd clearly show some of the detail revealed in this way. Use of this technique has been essential to understanding the fish bone evidence from this site.
