Timeline: Mesopotamian Seal Engraving

Cylinder seals were made in Mesopotamia between around 3500 BC and 400 BC. However, different techniques were used throughout this period.

Prior to 1750 BC : Designs on hard stone seals were mainly chipped.

Between 1750 BC and 1300 BC : Designs on hard stone seals were often filed or drilled.

After 1300 BC: Designs on the hard stone seals were mainly wheel cut.
