Dier el-Bersha

The rock cut tombs found on the the east bank of the Nile at Dier el-Bersha formed the cemetery for the city of Khemenu. Its Discovery in the 19th Centuary revealed a series of large decorated tombs, containing governors of the Hare nome province, dating back to the 11th and 12th Dynasties (2000-1800 BC).
