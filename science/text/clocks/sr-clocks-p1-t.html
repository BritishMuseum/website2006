Iron and Steel in Clocks and Watches

Clocks and watches have become an important part of our lives over the past 500 years. The British Museum has a large and comprehensive collection which is being studied to learn more about the types of metals used and how these have changed over time. High performance, and hence high quality, metal components were required for clocks and watches. Since we know when most of the clocks were made, we can use scientific analysis to learn more about the state-of-the-art technologies and materials of their day.
