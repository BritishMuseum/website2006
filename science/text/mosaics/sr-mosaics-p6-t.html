Turquoise Mosaics from Mexico

The tesserae were stuck onto the wood using resin, as shown in this "edge-on" view down the microscope.

Under the microscope, we have seen tiny flecks of gold on the moulded black resin that forms the pointed rattles on each of the snakes - originally their rattles would have been gold.
