National Science Week 17-25th March 2001

The British Museum has created an exciting programme of events for National Science Week 2001 with hands-on workshops, demonstrations, behind the scenes visits, gallery talks and a science fair! Join the Museum's scientists, conservators and curators as they use science to reveal the secrets of the amazing collections in the Museum.

Events in National Science Week have been organised by the British Museum Education Department as part of the Glaxo Wellcome Science Education programme.
