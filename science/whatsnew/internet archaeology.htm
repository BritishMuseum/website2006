Production and Distribution of Pottery
in the Eastern Mediterranean

Volume 9 of Internet Archaeology, a themed issue edited by Louise Joyner from the Department of Scientific Research at the British Museum, is now available online and can be found at: http://intarch.ac.uk/journal/issue9/index.html.

For free subscription details please see http://intarch.ac.uk.

This collection of seven papers brings together the latest research on pottery from the Eastern Mediterranean region. The papers address issues surrounding the production and distribution of ceramics dating from the Neolithic to Medieval periods, using petrographic techniques. Colour images of pottery fabrics taken under the microscope have been used extensively to illustrate these papers; one article has colour images that illustrate the optical effects when the thin section is rotated on the microscope stage.
