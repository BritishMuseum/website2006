The Science and Conservation of Treasure:
A Study Day to explore the world of gold treasures found in Britain

Thursday March 11th 2004 9.30 - 17.00. Clore Education Centre

To be held in conjunction with the Treasure exhibition and National Science week , explores many aspects of the archaeology, science and conservation of gold treasures from Britain from the Bronze Age to the Civil War.

Tickets £24, Concessions £18, to include entry to the exhibition on the day, details and booking forms from:

P.T. Craddock, The British Museum, London, WC1B 3DG
