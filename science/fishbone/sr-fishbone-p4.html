The Study of Ancient Animal Bone?

The analysis of the fish remains from Ra's al-Hadd shows that people ate a range of species, including tuna, jack, pompano, scad, rockcod, grouper, parrotfish and queenfish. These could be caught by line-fishing from boats or with nets. Artefactual evidence of copper fish-hooks, traces of line and a net-sinker supports these suggestions.
