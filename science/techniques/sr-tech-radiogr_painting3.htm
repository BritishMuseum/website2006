From the x-rays it is clear that what originally appeared to be a single piece was in fact made from two separate wall paintings joined togeather and set into a block of plaster, with a "seam" between them. To give more strength to the block a number of pieces of scrap metal were laid across the join between the two paintings and also along two cracks in the individual panels.

The metal objects seem to be a collection of the sort of oddments which might be found in any workshop; there is a broken saw blade, a broken file, and a number metal offcuts.
