How was an Enamel like this made?

The plaque is a sheet of copper. First the picture of Naaman and his servants was engraved in the copper. Then coloured glass was ground up and placed in the cells (small hollows) which had been carved out. See the engraving using an X-ray of the plaque

The plaque was then placed into an oven at red heat so that the coloured glasses melted in to place. A design made by melting glass onto metal in this way is known as an enamel. But where did the coloured glass come from.
