Events

  Join in some of our many events intended to bring people of all ages
'closer to the master'.
  Through lectures and panel discussions with leading authorities
you can learn more about the genius of Michelangelo as a sculptor,
painter, architect and poet.
  Among other events celebrating Michelangelo's life and times are
a season of films and documentaries, a concert of Renaissance
music, a lively Points of View day, practical drawing and sculpture
and a Michelangelo Mega Draw for all the family.
  There are also free presentations introducing the exhibition and
free screenings of a special film on Michelangelo presented by
the Director of the British Museum
