Workshops and study session


  Friday 23 June, 10.00-17.00
Colonnade
   

Imagining Michelangelo: stone carving
This workshop introduces beginners to sorts of tools and materials used
by Michelangelo in his sculpture. The day offers a rare practical chance to
learn the techniques of stone carving and how this great Renaissance
sculptor may have worked away at stone, including marble.
In association with Arts Express.
£30, BM Friends and concessions £25
Booking required, 15 places
You will be working outside and are advised to wear old clothes.
