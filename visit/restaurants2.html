The Court Restaurant
A unique dining experience

The Court Restaurant, located under the acclaimed Norman Foster glass roof in the Great Court of the British Museum, offers a choice of dishes from around the world.  Enjoy light lunches, traditional afternoon teas and  evening meals with a comprehensive range of wines and spirits and experience friendly waiter service at the heart of one of the world's greatest museums.
