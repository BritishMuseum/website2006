On 6 December 2000, Her Majesty The Queen will conduct the formal opening of the Queen Elizabeth II Great Court, the £100 million millennium project which has created London's spectacular new public covered courtyard.

The British Museum will exceptionally, be closed on the 6 December during the preparations for the historic opening of the new two-acre public space at the heart of the Museum.
