The Great Court's inaugural free exhibition displayed in the Joseph Hotung Great Court Gallery is kindly sponsored by Linklaters & Alliance.

Human Image is sponsored by

Human Image is an ambitious exhibition which covers a subject close to all of us, the representation of the human form.

Beginning with early artefacts from pre-historic Europe and ending in contemporary China and Nigeria, Human Image shows that the depiction and definition of the body have been of fundamental concern in the communication of ideas and information across all world cultures.

The exhibition draws material from across the Museum over time and across human civilizations. Themes, such as creation, devotion and power are included as well as a consideration of how artists have explored common themes.

A catalogue, exhibition guide and COMPASS virtual tour are available.
