A. G. Leventis Gallery of Cypriot Antiquities

Room 72 is the A. G. Leventis Gallery of Cypriot Antiquities. The position of Cyprus in the eastern Mediterranean has led to a rich mix of cultural influences on the island throughout its history. The objects in this gallery were all made or found in Cyprus, and range in date between about 4500BC and AD330. They illustrate Cypriot culture and civilisation from the earliest times to the end of the Roman period.
