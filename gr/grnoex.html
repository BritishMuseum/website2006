Warren Cup
Sex and society in Ancient Greece and Rome
11 May – 2 July 2006
Room 3, the Asahi Shimbun display. Admission free

The Warren Cup is a Roman silver drinking cup dating to
the 1st Century AD. It is decorated with sexual scenes showing two pairs of male lovers. To mark the publication of a new book on the cup by Dyfri Williams, this small exhibition offers a close focus on this drinking vessel and its cultural context.
