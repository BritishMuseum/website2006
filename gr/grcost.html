While each staff member is familiar with the collections as a whole, areas of specialisation include Greek sculpture and architecture; Roman sculpture, architecture and inscriptions; Greek vases and terracottas, Roman pottery; Cypriot archaeology; Greek and Roman glass; Greek and Roman bronzes; Italic archaeology and pre-Roman Italy; the cultures of the Greek Bronze Age and the archaeology of the Roman Mediterranean.

The display, movement and storage of the collections is undertaken by a team of four Museum Assistants and three Mason's Assistants (dealing exclusively with the sculpture collections.)

Administrative support is provided by a team of five people, whose responsibilities include the management of the Departmental loan programme, the ordering of photographs and the running of the Departmental library.
