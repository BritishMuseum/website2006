Name Fishpool gold coin and jewellery hoard
Time period medieval (Wars of the Roses)
Institution British Museum
Place found Nottinghamshire

This is the largest hoard of gold coins ever found in Britain. The hoard, which also included some jewellery, was buried in Sherwood Forest, and was only discovered when some builders were at work in the 1960s. The hoard contained over 1237 gold coins and 10 pieces of gold jewellery. It was buried in about 1464 at a time of a terrible civil war in England and Wales to decide who would be King, known as the Wars of the Roses.
