Visit by Mrs Buthayna Hussain

PDF | Word

20-23 November 2003

Mrs Buthayna came to London on the 20 November and stayed till 23 November 2003. She met British Museum conservators, and in particular spent time with Pippa Pearce and Denise Ling (both from the Inorganic Section). She also met with Sarah Collins (Department of the Ancient Near East) and looked at the Ancient Near East stores and including the cuneiform tablet collection. The initial training programme and dates were discussed and agreed in principle. The three Iraqi conservators will arrive on the 28 January and stay till 26 March 2004.
