Throne of Weapons Tour
supported by the UK National Commission for UNESCO

As part of its commitment to Africa and to sharing its collections across the UK, the British Museum went on tour with the Throne of Weapons, made by Kester in Maputo, Mozambique in 2001.The sculpture comes out of the Transforming Arms into Tools project, supported by Christian Aid, which also produced the Tree of Life.
