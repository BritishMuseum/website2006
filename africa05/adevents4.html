Meet the artists
Art is at the centre of Africa at the British Museum.
Drop in this Autumn and meet practitioners Kader Keita, Magdalene Odundo and writer Diran Adebayo.

Kader Keita
Keita’s medium is traditional Bogolan, a heavy, cotton cloth of rust-coloured geometric designs made with river mud. Once the discipline of secret societies, it symbolises well-being and, today, diaspora identity.
