Welcome to Africa Now – ‘A summer programme of events for all ages celebrating contemporary African culture.

Dig deep and uncover connections between the continent’s diverse flora and cultures in the Ground Force Africa Garden.

Be creative – unleash your imagination with Family events in the Great Court and African Galleries.

Speak up – debate art and literature with novelist Diran Adebayo, artist Kader Keita and African director Kim Longinotto.

Beyond London, curator exchanges are assisting plans for the first African-led exhibition of Museum objects in Kenya.

The Throne of Weapons continues its tour from the G8 to Bristol for Black History Month.

And expanding online features are making the Africa collections available for interpretation across the world.
