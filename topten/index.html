What are the most important British treasures in the British Museum?
How do you decide if one treasure is more important than another?

The British Museum has been involved with two major BBC television programmes about British Treasures in 2002 and 2003.
In 'Our top ten treasures' Adam Hart Davies asked staff from the Museum to choice what they consider to be the most important British 'treasures' in the Museum and argue why.

In the 8 programmes of the series 'Hidden Treasure', Miranda Krestovnikoff follows the progress of major discoveries of ancient treasures. Staff from the Museum were involved in all programmes as one of the major tasks of the Museum is studying finds of Treasure (ancient gold and silver) found in Britain. Three of the discoveries featured in the series are now in the British Museum and will be amongst the star exhibits of the exhibition ' Buried Treasure: Finding Our Past'.
