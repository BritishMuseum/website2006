The Royal Game of Ur

  

20 July – 3 September 2006
The Asahi Shimbun Display, Room 3
Admission free

  

Come and find out how to play one of the oldest known games in the World. The Royal Game of Ur was played in one of the oldest cities on earth in what is today southern Iraq. See the game, learn more about and even play it yourself.

 

Opening times
Daily 10.00 – 17.30
Open late Thursday & Friday until 20.30

  

Sculptures in the Great Court

  
18 May - 3 September 2006
Admission free 		 
In addition to the work featured in Room 35, a specially commissioned sculpture by well known Iraqi artist Dia al-Azzawi will be in the Great Court along with other sculptures by Iranian artist Parviz Tanavoli.
