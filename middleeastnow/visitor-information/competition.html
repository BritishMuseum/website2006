Middle East Now season 18 May - 3 September 2006

You and a friend could be flying to Dubai, United Arab Emirates, for three nights and staying in the Jumeirah Beach Hotel, recently awarded ‘Best hotel in the Middle East*’. Staying in one of the 598 5-star rooms, you can relax and enjoy the hotel’s 23 restaurants and bars, the luxurious spa and leisure facilities and exclusive private beach.


  
Highlights

  

Outside the hotel, and away from the towering skyscrapers and world famous shopping of the city, Dubai is a treasure trove of eclectic sights and sounds. Scour the souks for a bargain, visit the majestic sand dunes or tour the historical sites and traditional museums. The collection of the Dubai Museum focuses on Arabic language and culture, and Sheikh Saeed Al Maktoum House is now a Museum of Historical Photographs and Documents of the Emirate of Dubai.

Dubai Holding is proud to partner with the British Museum on the Middle East Now season and Word into Art, the first major exhibition to explore the artistic promise of the Middle East and calligraphy as an art form. Dubai Holding is the major success story of the United Arab Emirates, operating in 11 life-improving industries such as healthcare, media and communications and are committed to promoting and preserving the artistic heritage of the region.
