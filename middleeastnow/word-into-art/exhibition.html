Online Exhibition

"The British Museum has worked in partnership with Birzeit University in Palestine, and with the artists themselves, to make an online version of the Word into Art exhibition available in the Birzeit Virtual Gallery, in both English and Arabic. The virtual exhibition will continue to be available to students and lovers of contemporary Middle Eastern art long after the temporary exhibition in the Museum has closed."
Neil MacGregor, Director
