Workshop

  

Persian Word Art (for adults)

  

Saturday 27 May, 14.00–17.00
Sackler Rooms
£15, concessions £12
15 places

  

Learn with artist Jila Peacock about the history of calligraphy
in the Islamic world and watch a demonstration of the
alphabet and the nasta’liq style of writing, before trying
your own hand with a practical session on calligraphy
using pen, ink and paper.
