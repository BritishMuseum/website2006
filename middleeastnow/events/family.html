Arabesque half-term event

  

Monday 29 May – Saturday 3 June
11.00 – 16.00 daily
Great Court
Admission free, just drop in

  

From Sumer to Syria, explore the treasures of the Museum’s collection (including Iraq’s Royal Game of Ur and objects in the Islamic Gallery). Discover the wonders of the Arab World - its amazing patterns, places and people through art workshops, storytelling, music and games inspired by the Middle East. Please click here to view the full programme.

 

Middle East Family Summer

  

1, 3, 8, 10, 15, 17, 22, 24 August
11.00 – 16.00 daily
Admission free, just drop in

  

Enjoy a feast of Middle Eastern entertainment. Pit your wits against the opposition in the Royal Game of Ur and backgammon or thrill to gripping tales of heroes, genies, riddles and kings. On the same dates there will be
storytelling sessions for adults.
