Middle East Family Summer
1, 3, 8, 10, 15, 17, 22, 24 August
11.00 – 16.00 daily
Admission free, just drop in

Enjoy a feast of Middle Eastern entertainment.
Pit your wits against the opposition by playing
the Royal Game of Ur and backgammon or be
entranced by gripping tales of heroes, genies,
riddles and kings. On the same dates there will
be storytelling sessions for adults.
