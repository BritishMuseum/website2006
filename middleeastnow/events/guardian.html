Religion, politics and sexuality: What can history tell us about the boundaries of expression in the Middle East now?

Chaired by Jon Snow
Tuesday 13 June
Refreshments and private view from19.00
Debate 20.00-21.30
Sold Out

Religion, politics and sexuality are contested subjects within artistic expression and also raise fundamental questions about boundaries and taboos in art. A panel of international speakers look at the history and contemporary practice of artists of the Middle East and debate these issues.
