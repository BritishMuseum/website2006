Corridor of Commerce
Archaeology and the lives of the
peoples of the Gulf over 7000 year

  

Thursday 27 July, 18.30   

In this illustrated lecture, Dr Derek Kennet, archaeologist
and lecturer at the University of Durham, will explore how the
pivotal position of the Gulf from Antiquity to the 19th century
is reflected in the rich archaeological heritage of this region

£5, concessions £3
Followed by refreshments
