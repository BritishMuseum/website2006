Lecture: Between the Image and the Word: shape poems in Persian calligraphy
Jila Peacock
Friday 26 May, 13.00
Stevenson Lecture Theatre, admission free
Artist Jila Peacock has printed a hand-made book of shape poems from Hafez of Shiraz, the great poet of medieval Persia. She explains the importance of Hafez and describes the development of figural calligraphy.
