An exploration of the symbolic power of badges and other similar objects to express identity and belonging, belief and dissent.

A badge is an unspoken message: an expression of identity, a signal of membership, a declaration of loyalty or a statement of belief. The message can be subtle or direct, serious or humorous, and can provoke a variety of reactions from approval and respect to fear and hostility.
