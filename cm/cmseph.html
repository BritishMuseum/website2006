We have a new online picture library at www.bmimages.com. This website enables picture researchers, buyers and members of the general public, to search for images of objects held in the Museum's collections and to buy high resolution digital images online using a credit card. Customers who wish to buy digital images are advised to first of all look at www.bmimages.com to see if the image is available on the website – if so they can complete the purchase and download the image all via the web site using a credit card as a payment method.

If the image is not available on the website but you know it is of an object that is in the collections of the Coins and Medals Department, then please email us at - cmphoto@thebritishmuseum.ac.uk

If the image you require is on the website, but you would rather order traditional images (print or transparency) it may be possible to provide such formats for you. You should contact the Coins and Medals email address above for advice and costings. Please note that as the Museum moves over to completely digital supply, such image types are being phased out. If available, they will take at least 28 days to be supplied.
