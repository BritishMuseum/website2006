research > publications          
 

The Department has an impressive publication record, covering subjects as diverse as medals of the Italian Renaissance and Romano-British coin hoards. In addition to writing numerous articles for both academic and popular periodicals and journals on coins and medals, members of the department write, edit and contribute to more expansive works on history and archaeology. The Department has followed a policy of publishing scholarly catalogues of its extensive collections since the early 1800s.
