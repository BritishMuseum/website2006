The collection and library are accessible to all members of the public. They can be consulted by appointment in the Department's Study Room at the following times:

Mornings
Monday to Friday 10.00-13.00 (except Wednesday)

Afternoons
Monday to Friday 14.15-16.00

Visitors applying for admission must produce evidence of identification (for non-UK visitors this must be a passport). Visitors wishing to consult the curator of a particular area of the collection are advised to make an appointment in advance.
