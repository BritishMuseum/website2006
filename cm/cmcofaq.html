Can I have objects identified and valued at the department?
yes, enquiries can be made in person, by telephone, post or email.

Where are coins and medals on display in the museum?
The majority of objects are in Gallery 68 and the temporary exhibition space Gallery 69a.
There are also objects from the collection of King George III in the Enlightenment Gallery.
See Floor Plans of the Museum.

Can I see objects that are not on display?
Yes, by visiting the Study Room in the department.

Can I order photographs?
Photos can be ordered through the British Museum Photographic Department.

How can I report potential treasure finds?
Contact your local Finds Liaison Officer through the Portable Antiquities Website
