Photographs of the Collection

We can only provide images of the objects that are in our own collection. We do not have any Royal Mummies, or any of the artefacts from the Tomb of Tutankhamun; these are all in the Egyptian Museum Cairo. We cannot provide any images of sites, monuments or tombs that are in Egypt, modern or archive images of Egypt, or Egyptological personalities such as Howard Carter, or objects from other museum collections. We are also unable to undertake picture research.

We do not have a visual database of the Egyptian collection, but you may find COMPASS useful for research. This is an online database of around 5000 objects, chosen from all the Museum collections, and is part of the Museum website; the address is www.thebritishmuseum.ac.uk/compass

All images from the Museum are supplied on special order, and can take up to 6-8 weeks to supply. When we receive your order, which should be sent to us as far in advance of your deadline as possible, it should include the following:

Your full postal address
Fax and telephone number
Format required (colour transparency, BW print, etc.)
Size of image required
Contact details if payment is from a different source
Date of deadline
Title of publication
Date of publication
Photocopies or small digital images for identification of the objects
