Sackler Lecture and Colloquium 2005

The Raymond and Beverly Sackler Foundation Distinguished Lecture in Egyptology 2005: 'Piramesses - Capital of Ramesses the Great and Portal to the Eastern Mediterranean'

To be delivered by Dr Edgar Pusch, formerly of the Roemer und Pelizaeus-Museum, Hildesheim, and Director of the excavation at Piramesses-Qantir.

Wednesday 13 July 2005 at 18.00

The lecture will be followed on 14 and 15 July by the Annual International Egyptological Colloquium, entitled 'Egypt and the Hittites: contacts, conflict and diplomacy in the Late Bronze Age.'
