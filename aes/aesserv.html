Services

The Department offers a number of services to the public.

We are open for public enquiries, either in person or by phone, Monday-Friday, 10am - 1pm, 2pm - 4.30pm. Access to the Department is via the landing on the East Stairs. Alternatively, one can write to or email the Department. Object identification forms part of this service.

Access, by appointment, is available to archival material, relating to the collection and the wider study of Egyptian and Sudanese antiquities. Objects from the reserve collection can be viewed, again by appointment

Photographs of objects in the collection, in Black & White, or colour, are available through the Department of Photography and Imaging.

Tours of the reserve collection are occasionally offered to groups of students and societies, including the British Museum Friends. School parties should contact the Education Department, for details on events related to Ancient Egypt and Sudan.

The Department pursues a programme of international and domestic loans for exhibitions related to the cultures of Ancient Egypt and Sudan.
