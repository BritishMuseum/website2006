Photographic

We have a new online picture library at www.bmimages.com. This website was launched in February 2006 and enables picture researchers and buyers to search for images of objects held in the Museum's collections and to buy high resolution digital images online using a credit card. To search, buy and download can take as little as ten minutes if you are buying online.

If the image is not available on www.bmimages.com but you know it is of an object that is in the Department of Ancient Egypt and Sudan collection then please contact us directly.

If the image is available on the website but you need it in traditional format (print or transparency) it may be possible, in exceptional circumstances, to provide these formats for you by special order but please bear in mind that this service is in the process of being phased out as we move over to Digital Format. Also, these images will take at least 28 days to be supplied.

Please note that the Department of Ancient Egypt and Sudan can only supply images of the collection. We cannot supply photographs of Egyptian artefacts in other museums, monuments in Egypt, old photographic views of Egypt, modern views of Egypt, the treasures of Tutankhamun, and Egyptological personalities.

A specialist picture library should be approached for such material.
