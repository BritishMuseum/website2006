The Prints and Drawings Gallery (Room 90) is regularly updated with new and innovative exhibitions. Recent displays have included works by such renowned artists as Rembrandt and Picasso, while Michelangelo's beautiful cartoon Epifania is permanently on display here. The museum holds approximately three million works on paper, dating from the 15th century to the present day, including Old Master prints and drawings, satires of the 18th and 19th centuries and other important works, ensuring that there is an ever changing backdrop for events held in this gallery. Please telephone the Corporate Services Office for information on current and forthcoming exhibitions
