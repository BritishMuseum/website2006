The recently opened Sainsbury African Gallery (Room 25) is a striking venue that reveals African culture in all its colour and diversity. Dramatically arranged and lit, the three rooms that comprise this gallery house exhibits from all areas of Africa's arts and crafts, from pottery and metal forging to woodcarving and weaving. In these intriguing surroundings there is much to capture the attention of guests, where specially tailored events can be arranged for up to 300 guests.
