Door dimensions:

For health and safety reasons, it is essential to liaise with the Corporate Services Office regarding any large items (eg sets or lighting rigs) needing to be moved in or out of the conference facilities.

Additional power requirements can be provided by prior arrangement.  For health and safety reasons, all power requirements must be cleared with the Corporate Services Office prior to an event.


Exhibition stands / floor supported flats:

Exhibition stands (up to a maximum of 2.5m high) are permitted within the seminar rooms or foyers only.  Floor supported flats up to a height of 4m/13ft are permitted in specific locations by prior arrangement.  The Foyer ceiling height is 4.53m/14.86ft.


Induction loops:

Induction loops are fitted in all lecture theatres and seminar rooms.  Please note that the induction loop system is fed from the PA microphone. For seminar room loop operation a radio microphone will need to be provided, even if the PA system is not in use.

Internet connection points:

Internet connection points can be provided on request at an additional cost, by prior arrangement only (minimum notice of 4 weeks is required). 

Lectern branding and dimensions:

Our brushed steel lecterns can be branded with your company logo by using lightweight card or similar (client must arrange design and printing) attached with >blue-tac= or sellotape.  Adhesive velcro must not be used on the lecterns as it marks the surface.

The front piece of the lecterns in both theatres measures 93cm (width) by 99cm (height) - this is the maximum size for branding.  In addition, there is a 20cm high recessed foot section at the base.

Reception desk facilities:

The reception desk in the Clore Education Centre is equipped with one fax machine and two telephones with outside lines.  Photocopying facilities are available on request (a charge may apply for bulk copying).
