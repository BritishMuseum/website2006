Jury's Great Russell Street Hotel

A superb location in the heart of central London. Standing proud on Great Russell Street, just two minutes walk from The British Museum, this 4 star hotel has on its doorstep unrivalled shopping in Oxford / Bond Street, and is convenient to Covent Garden, Theatreland and the City's financial district. Tottenham Court Road tube station is a 2 minutes walk away.

In 1998 the building was purchased by the Jurys Doyle Hotel Group for restoration and conversion to a unique four star hotel. Original Lutyens design has been retained throughout the major interior spaces, with the grandeur of the reception lobby and the magnificent meeting rooms setting the tone for this special hotel. Lutyens inspired décor and furniture design is faithfully reflected throughout the building.
