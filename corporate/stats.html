Statistics

Visitors
1.4 million UK visitors
4.2 million international visitors, contributing to the growth of London's tourism
50 official visits by VIPs during the year
More than 1.5 million visits to the British Museum website, and 12.5 million hits
100 trained volunteers conducted 7 daily eyeOpener tours for more than 25,000 visitors, an increase of 30%

Education
1,000 schoolchildren visit every day during term time
Sponsored new posts created in Education, to develop Families, Science, and Arab world programmes
Over 20,000 visitors used the departmental students' rooms, and over 20,000 postal enquiries answered

Exhibitions
4 exhibitions toured in the UK to Aberdeen, Bath, Brighton, Durham, Exeter, Glasgow, Llandudno, Manchester, Ulster, Wolverhampton
7 exhibitions toured internationally
6,000 objects added to the collections, including 123 of special significance
9,063 objects lent within the UK to 96 venues; 2,215 objects lent to 75 international venues

Publishing
One of the largest museum publishers in the world with 50-60 new books each year by British Museum Press
In addition to the many resource packs for teachers, exhibition and gallery guides produced, staff published some 220 articles in scholarly publications

Expertise
37 excavations undertaken or contributed to 8,500 objects plus 4,769 coins worked
on by the Conservation Department
200 reports on artefacts in the Museum's collections compiled by the Department of Scientific Research
223 Treasure Act cases processed, involving between one or several hundred objects; scientific analysis and a written report produced for each

Funding
£97.9 million funding for the Great Court scheme completed
Contributions from the British Museum Society rose by 35% ­ total contribution exceeded £1 million
