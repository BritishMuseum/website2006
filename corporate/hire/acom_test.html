Jury's Great Russell Street Hotel
A superb location in the heart of central London. Standing proud on Great Russell Street, just two minutes walk from The British Museum, this 4 star hotel has on its doorstep unrivalled shopping in Oxford / Bond Street, and is convenient to Covent Garden, Theatreland and the City's financial district. Tottenham Court Road tube station is a 2 minutes walk away.

The Grange White Hall Hotel
With it's unique location overlooking the British Museum on Montague Street, The Grange White Hall Hotel provides sumptuous comfort in one of the worlds most fashionable and exciting neighbourhoods. The private landscaped gardens of the Grange White Hall Hotel and Bloomsbury Townhouse Collection enable you to enjoy a peaceful haven within the vibrant surroundings of the West end, the City's financial centre and the atmospheric Covent Garden.

The Montague on the Gardens
The Montague on the Gardens, located adjacent to The British Museum, is one of an exclusive collection of boutique, luxury hotels which are located in London, Geneva, Palm Beach and Cape Town - part of Red Carnation Hotels, who have a special relationship as a preferred partner of The British Museum.
