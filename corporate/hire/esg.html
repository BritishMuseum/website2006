An event in the Egyptian Sculpture Gallery (Room 4) offers
you and your guests exclusive access to the finest collection of Egyptian artefacts outside Egypt, including the famous Rosetta Stone, whose inscriptions made the decipherment of ancient Egyptian hieroglyphs possible. Other significant exhibits include the Giant Scarab, an enduring symbol of ancient Egypt, and a statue of Rameses II, one of the largest pieces of sculpture in the Museum.

The room itself is impressively spacious, making it one of the Museum's most popular venues for receptions or dinners.
