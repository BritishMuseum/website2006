The impressive galleries at The British Museum are perfect for holding prestigious receptions and dinners. The opportunity to entertain in the galleries is reserved for Corporate Partners and sponsors of the museum only, thereby ensuring exclusivity for the joining company and enabling us to build excellent working partnerships. In addition to this privileged access Corporate Partners and major sponsors are entitled to a whole range of client and staff benefits including free entry to paying exhibitions, behind-the-scenes tours and curator talks in the work place.
