Located on the upper terrace of the Great Court, overlooking the Reading Room, the Court Restaurant provides guests with an intimate dining or reception space nestling under the Great Court's colossal but delicately designed glass and steel roof. The restaurant is a perfect venue for receptions up to 200 guests or seated dinners for up to 150 guests, and is available for private events on Sunday, Monday, Tuesday and Wednesday evenings.

Exclusive tours of the Hotung Exhibition Gallery can be arranged during these times. The restaurant is also available for private breakfasts throughout the week. An additional fee may be payable for weekend events.
