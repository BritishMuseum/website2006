Catering / Other Services
These are not included in the venue hire fee. Any of our accredited suppliers may be employed for gallery events, with the exception of events taking place in gallery 17 where the accredited service providers list is restricted. Please ask for details.

Within the Court Restaurant, only Digby Trout may cater for an event.

Deposit, Contract and Insurance
Following a written quotation from the Conference Office, clients are required to pay a non-refundable deposit (50% of the total venue hire) to secure their booking and to sign a contract.

The client is obliged to provide proof of cover for Public Liability insurance to the value of £5 million. Please ask the Conference Office if you would like details of companies who can arrange this.

Additional Opportunities
Private views of special exhibitions and gallery talks by Museum curators may also be arranged.

British Museum branded gifts and books may be purchased at a discounted rate as part of a Corporate Event. Please ask the Corporate Services Office for details.

The British Museum Company can open any of its shops specifically as part of a corporate event
by prior arrangement.

Parking
Parking spaces for disabled drivers or VIP guests may be supplied on site by prior arrangement with the Conference Office (usually up to a maximum of 4 cars).

There are several NCP car parks in the vicinity of the Museum and discounted rates for block bookings may be available. click here for details.
