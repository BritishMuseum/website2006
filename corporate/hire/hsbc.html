The HSBC Money Gallery (Room 68) is home to an exciting collection which illustrates how money has been made and used throughout the world during the last 4,500 years. The gallery also contains the UK's national collection of coins, medals and paper money, and is a formal, elegant venue for small receptions.

Receptions in the HSBC Money Gallery

Maximum capacity: 100 standing
Smoking is not permitted anywhere within the Museum. Dancing and recorded/amplified music are not permitted in the galleries but may be permitted in the Great Court by arrangement.
