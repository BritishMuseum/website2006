The Chesterfield Mayfair
In exclusive Mayfair, just off Berkeley Square, the intimate 4 star Chesterfield Hotel is renowned for combining the gracious living standards of an elegant past with state-of-the-art business and leisure convenience. Just a short stroll from the fashionable shops on Bond and Regent Streets, and close to Green Park and the sights of Piccadilly Circus and Leicester Square.

The deluxe Suites and luxurious guest rooms are air-conditioned and beautifully appointed with distinctive fabrics. Combined with thoughtful extras, including plush bathrobes, slippers, still and sparkling mineral water and welcome gift as well as generous desk space, voicemail, and fax and modem lines for those on business, we ensure that you experience the utmost in comfort and style.The award-winning restaurant offers superb cuisine, including 'the best grilled Dover Sole in London' and 'Sommerlier' selections of great wines from the Wine Room. The Consevatory, serving delicious afternoon teas, offers a relaxed and tranquil atmosphere, and the bar is one of London's most comfortable to unwind over a favourite drink.

The Milestone Hotel & Apartments
Escape to a world of luxury at the 5 star Milestone Hotel & Apartments; recently awarded the Commitment to Quality Award by Leading Hotels of the World as the Best Hotel in Europe and the prestigious London Tourism Award for Hotel of the Year (under 60 bedrooms). Built in the late 19th Century as two gracious townhouses and splendidly restored, retaining many of the period details and original architectural features, The Milestone offers the ultimate in style and elegance, with unrivalled panoramic views of Kensington Palace.

Each of the guest rooms and beautifully and individually designed with antique furnishings, marble baths, air conditioning and the latest technology. Thoughtful extras include a welcome drink, fresh fruit and flowers in all rooms and a selection of soaps for you to choose, as well as a request for your personal preferences, before arrival, from choice of duvet to choice of pillows. A 24 hour personal butler service is available to all guests staying in Suites.

Enjoy the intimate restaurant, Cheneston's, where you will experience the finest in European cuisine and personal service; relax with casual dining in the Conservatory where natural floods in, take afternoon tea in the Park Lounge and unwind in Stables Bar - the perfect end to an evening. The Milestone also has a health club with free weights and cardiovascular equipment, a sauna and whirlpool.

The Rubens at the Palace
With a unique 'royal' location opposite Buckingham Palace, The Rubens is dedicated to providing superb personal service in luxurious and stylish surroundings, amidst London's famous sites; just moments from Victoria Station and a short walk to St James's Park, Big Ben, Flight 360 - the London Eye - and The Palace of Westminster. This 4 star hotel's guest rooms and suites are all air-conditioned and equipped with every modern convenience including in-room fax, USA/UK modem lines, special touches such as complimentary bottled mineral water, tea and coffee making facilities and plush bathrobes and slippers. The Royal Wing features eight magnificent rooms, themed after Kings and Queens of Great Britain. Sumptuously furnished with hi-tech facilities and stunning marble bathrooms.

International Cuisine is served in the intimacy of the Library Restaurant, as well as tempting selections from the Old Master's Carvery. The Palace Lounge and Cavalry Bar are perfect locations to meet friends and unwind.
