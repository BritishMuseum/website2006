The Montague on the Gardens

The Montague on the Gardens, located adjacent to the British Museum, is one of an exclusive collection of boutique, luxury hotels which are located in London, Geneva, Palm Beach and Cape Town - part of Red Carnation Hotels.

The Montague is perfectly positioned for visitors to the British Museum, just seconds away, and provides a tranquil haven to relax and unwind.  This unique, four-star hotel, renowned for its great service, overlooks secluded private gardens and feels like a fine country house in the heart of London.  With regular live jazz music, summer barbeques on the wood-deck and an intimate, traditional style fitted with modern business technology, the staff look forward to welcoming you to the Montague experience - a ‘home away from home’.
