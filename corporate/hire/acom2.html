The Bloomsbury Townhouse Collection
Is a charming collection of 18th century Georgian townhouse hotels which have been beautifully and immaculately restored.  Sophisticated design and décor add to the unique charm of these boutique hotels, all of which provide a stylish home from home in an ideal central London location.

The townhouses are all situated within walking distance from each other, in the heart of London's literary Bloomsbury, adjacent to the British Museum and moments away from Covent Garden, Oxford Circus, the West ends myriad attractions.  The Grange White Hall Hotel is also within the Collection.
