Welcome to The British Museum Film Society.

Come to The British Museum on the first Friday and enjoy the best in world cinema.

The inspirational setting of The British Museum will become a backdrop for all who love cinema.

The films are screened in the BP Lecture Theatre in the Clore Education Centre at 18.30. A pay bar will be available after the film.

An annual subscription of £30 (£25 concessions) covers admission to all 12 films at no extra cost. Members may bring a guest for £4 (£3 concessions).
