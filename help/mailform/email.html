Information Request: E-Mail Address Field

You may fill in these fields, but you do not have to unless you want to. As it is difficult quickly to validate an email address, and to check that it can be delivered to, we have to ask you to enter it twice, so that you are certain that you have entered a valid e-mail address. Museum staff may then send requested information to that e-mail address, if it is appropriate to do so.
