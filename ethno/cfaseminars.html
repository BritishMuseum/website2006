Centre for Anthropology, British Museum
New seminar series
The British Museum Centre for Anthropology is organising a new seminar series on current debates in anthropology, broadly focused on ideas of the visual and the material. We are seeking to encourage the use of the resources offered by the Centre and hope that this seminar series will provide a forum for the wider anthropological community.
