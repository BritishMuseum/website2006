services          
 



The Department of Africa, Oceania and the America's Collections Enquiry Service and Anthropology Library offer the following services:

1) Access to a selection of photographs of objects in the collections: Stored by continent, the black and white photo files represent some 10% of the objects in the collection. There are also some slides and 5" x 4" transparencies but not as many as black and white.

2) Access to copies of Accession Registers and the computerised database of the collections. Help is on hand to explain these resources which often exhibit complicated registration numbers or computer notation.

3) Access to other supplementary ethnographical documents relating to the collections.

4) Personal advice about the Department, its collections and its exhibitions etc.

5) The availability of a selection of general books pertinent to the study of the Department's collections or the subject matter covered by ethnographic study.

6) A selection of educational resources.

7) Receipt and processing of objects or collections for purchase or donation.

8) A selection of bibliographies on ethnographic topics.
