Centre for Anthropology, British Museum

The Ethnography Department of the British Museum has returned to Bloomsbury and, under the banner of the Centre for Anthropology, the anthropology library and collections enquiries service has recommenced.

The Centre is located inside the north entrance of the British Museum, Bloomsbury.
