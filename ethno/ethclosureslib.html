The Anthropology Library of the British Museum's Ethnography Department has been closed since 15th September 2003. It will re-open to the public as part of the new Centre for Anthropology, in the British Museum, Bloomsbury, near the north entrance, on 10th May 2004.

Fellows of the Royal Anthropological Insititute (RAI) will be able to use the Library from 13th April 2004.

Fellows of the RAI will be able to keep books on loan over the closure period; Junior Fellows, however, are required to return all items from loan before 15th September. Any Fellow may return books by post or in person to the front hall desk of 6 Burlington Gardens, London W1S 3EX after closure. After closure, recorded information only will be available on the Burlington Gardens Reading Room extension 020 7323 8031.

Brief enquiries may be directed to:
AnthropologyLibrary@thebritishmuseum.ac.uk

Please note we will not be able to conduct detailed research for readers or accept photocopying orders.

The library catalogue is unfortunately not yet available on the internet.

Further information and updates will be posted on our website. We apologise for the inconvenience the closure will cause. However, the new location in Bloomsbury will offer both RAI Fellows and the public a better service and we look forward to welcoming both to the Centre for Anthropology in Spring 2004.
