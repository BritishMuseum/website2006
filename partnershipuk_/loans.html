We organise the loan of thousands of our objects to other museums in the UK every year. Objects are borrowed by museums for display in temporary exhibitions and also lent as a longer term resource for new galleries. Over 2002-3 we are lending over 536 objects in 22 loans to non-national museums and 146 objects in 11 loans to nationals.
