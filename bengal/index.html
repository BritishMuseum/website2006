Durga | Myths of Bengal | The art of peace Programme of Events | Visitor Information

See the rich cultural heritage of Bengal come alive this Autumn at the British Museum, through a range of exhibitions, displays and events, including:

Durga: Creating an image of the goddess Witness the creation of a spectacular image of the goddess Durga, created by Bengali craftsmen using straw, clay and paint

Myths of Bengal Explore the rich oral tradition of Bengal in an exhibition that explores the stories of the region through the Museum's beautiful painted scrolls

The art of peace: Paintings by the poet Tagore Experience, for the first time in public, the paintings of the Nobel Prize-winning poet, Rabindranath Tagore, in this introduction to the artist

There is also a vast array of events, lectures, films and workshops that investigate the diverse culture of this vibrant region.
