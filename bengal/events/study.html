Study Day

Tagore Study Day

Saturday 14 October, 09.30-16.00
BP Lecture Theatre

A day on the extraordinary life and work of Tagore, including Dr Ketaki Kushari Dyson, poet and writer William Radice and composer Naresh Sohal. Includes a screening of Ray's documentary on Tagore and a music and dance performance.

09.30-10.00    
Registration

10.00-10.05    
Welcome and introduction by Richard Blurton, British Museum

10.05-11.00
William Radice, SOAS, on Tagore’s poetry

11.00-11.30
Coffee

11.30 -12.15
Naresh Sohal on Tagore and music

12.15-13.15
Akademi dance performance

13.15-14.30
Lunch, please make your own arrangements

14.30-15.15    
Dr Dyson, Tagore painting and colour

15.15-16.00    
Prof. Anisuzzaman, Dhaka University, Tagore in Bangladesh

16.00-16.50    
Screening of Ray’s Rabindranath Tagore, 54 minutes,1961
This documentary, commissioned by the Government of India, is a portrait of the life and times of India’s most celebrated poet and was made to celebrate the centenary of Tagore’s birth.

16.50
Closing remarks

The programme may be subject to change.
