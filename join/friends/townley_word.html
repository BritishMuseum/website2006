Welcome to the Townley Group

"Since it was launched in 1997 the Townley Group has donated around £200,000 to specific Townley projects. This wonderful support comes from just 150 Townley contributors.

This year we have some new projects and some old favourites returned for further funding, but with new challenges. For the latter projects we can follow the progress year after year as more is understood and recorded by the curators and conservators.

It is heartening to know that we are instrumental in laying down the basis for future collections for generations to come. In our modest way, we follow in the footsteps of the most famous philanthropists and past supporters of this great Museum.

I commend the Townley Group to you".

Jeremy H Taylor
Chairman of the Townley Group


"By using our new website you can discover the many ways in which the Townley group has contributed to the work of the British Museum. Better still, by becoming a Townley you can effectively encourage the keepers in their work by contributing to the cost of the projects of your choice.

As Patron, I hope you will join us in this exiting undertaking".

Sir Simon Towneley
Patron of the Townley Group
