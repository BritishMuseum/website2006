'As Chairman of the British Museum Magazine Committee, I am delighted to introduce you to the Magazine of the Friends.

This is the Museum's only regular publication. It appears three times a year - Spring, Summer and Winter. Beautifully designed, printed on good quality paper, it is A4 in size and commonly runs to 44 or 48 pages.

Every Friend of the British Museum receives a copy of the magazine as part of their annual subscription. You can also subscribe to the magazine on its own, which is, perhaps, of particular interest to those living abroad who would like to keep up to date with the Museum, but are unable to be regular visitors. Or you can offer a subscription to a friend who would like to know more about the Museum.

I hope you will find the contents of the British Museum Magazine both stimulating and interesting. If you have any comments, please do let us know by email to the editor.'

Kate Trevelyan
Chair of the British Museum Magazine Committee

 

'The British Museum Magazine opens a window on to the breadth and depth of the British Museum's activities, and its place in the wider community.

Illustrated throughout in colour, the magazine gives extensive coverage not only of all the major exhibitions, but also of the Museum's other activities such as excavations, conservation, research and publications, providing an opportunity for curators to share their expertise and enthusiasm.

Particular prominence is given to recent acquisitions funded by the Friends, one of the most important ways in which the Friends contribute to the Museum.

Regular sections keep you up to date with news of the BM and of the Friends, and a comprehensive diary lists all the current and forthcoming exhibitions and displays.'

Anna Lethbridge
Editor
