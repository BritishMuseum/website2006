Events

Young Friends events are great fun! Recently Young Friends have travelled back to the Iron Age at Butser Ancient Farm, been through the Museum's basements and secret passages after hours and have made Japanese festival floats to take home. Young Friends can also come to our Sunday Clubs and learn about Egyptian food, African masks, Native American dream-catchers and Roman mosaics.

Would you dare to spend a night in The British Museum? As a Young Friend you will be able to come to one of our exciting sleepovers. You can bring your sleeping bag and a midnight feast and take part in lots of great activities before bedding down for the night among the sculptures and statues. We hold four sleepovers a year and each one is themed. Recent themes have included Egyptian, Amazonian, Oriental and Greek and Roman.

For details on our latest sleepovers, special events and Sunday Clubs please check the Events Diary page.

For more information about our events call 020 7323 8566 or email youngfriends@thebritishmuseum.ac.uk
