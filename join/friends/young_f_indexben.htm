Benefits

Join the Young Friends of the British Museum and enjoy:

  • Free entry with a guest to all exhibitions
  • Kids' magazine, ReMus, packed with fun, games and interesting articles (three times a year)
  • Special events
  • Sleepovers
  • Behind-the-scenes visits
  • Sunday Clubs
  • Competitions
  • Friends' & Family Rooms

Cost of membership: £17.50

Give a Young Friends' membership to a child!
