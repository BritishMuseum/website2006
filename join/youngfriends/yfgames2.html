Search the tomb of Tutankhamun for his lucky scarab

Tutankhamun has been buried in a tomb in the Valley of the Kings in Egypt, with all the things that his subjects think he night need in the afterlife. They have even put in a book of instructions known as the Book of the Dead. However, somebody has mislaid the lucky
scarab beetle, which was to bring him good fortune on his jouney, in the maze of his tomb. Can you search the tomb and see if you
can find a way in?
