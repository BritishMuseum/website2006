Nimrud Palace Reliefs

This room houses reliefs from the throne-room of Ashurnasirpal II in the Northwest Palace at Nimrud. These include a recessed panel with a double image of the king in a ritual scene which stood behind the throne and reliefs showing the king hunting and campaigning. There are also ritual scenes depicting the king and protective demons which come from other royal apartments.
