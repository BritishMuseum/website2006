25 June - 11 September 2005
Caravan Kingdoms: Yemen and the ancient incense trade

Arthur M. Sackler Gallery, Smithsonian Institution, Washington

Star objects from the British Museum's collection of ancient South Arabian antiquities loaned to this exhibition of over 200 objects from the National Museum in Sanaa (Yemen), the American Foundation for the Study of Man and the Dumbarton Oaks collection in Washington. An illustrated catalogue is available, and an online exhibition can be viewed at http://www.asia.si.edu/exhibitions/current/yemen.htm
