Access
The Department's services are located in the historic Arched Room. Access is via the large double doors at the north end of the Egyptian Sculpture Gallery (room 4), at the bottom of the West Stairs. Members of the public can be directed by information staff or find the Arched Room through the Visit Guide, which is available throughout the Museum.

Opening times for all services
The Department is open on weekdays 10.00 -13.00hrs - 14.00 -16.30hrs.

Study Room
Full details can be found on the ANE Study Room page.

General Enquiries
Can be made in person during the above times. Or see the contact information page to reach the department by any other method.
