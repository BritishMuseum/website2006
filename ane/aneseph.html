In February 2006, the Museum launched a new online picture library called BM Images. This website enables picture researchers, buyers and members of the general public, to search for images of objects held in the Museum's collections and to buy high resolution digital images online using a credit card.

Customers who wish to buy digital images are advised to first of all look at www.bmimages.com to see if the image is available on the website - if so they can complete the purchase and download the image all via the website using a credit card as a payment method. 

If the image is not available on the website but you know it is of an object that is in the Ancient Near Eastern collection  then please email us at ancientneareast@thebritishmuseum.ac.uk

If the image is available on the website but you want it in traditional format (print or transparency) then please contact the Department's Administrator at kwhenham@thebritishmuseum.ac.uk
