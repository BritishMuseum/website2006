Our Man in Persia: Objects collected by Sir Percy Sykes on his travels
Room 34, Case 7
Until December

This display focuses on Percy Sykes, explorer extraordinaire and historian of Iran. The Museum has Persian tiles and other objects acquired by Sykes which are complemented by objects lent by members of his family. See also the partner display in Room 34 From the heart of Persia: The photographs of Sir Percy and Gilbert Sykes (until September).
