services          
 

The Department offers access to its collections and expertise in as many ways as possible. Staff are pleased to assist with general enquiries and the identification of objects relating to the Department's collections.

Members of the public wishing to study some part of the collection are able to do so in person by visiting the departmental Study Room.

Photographic images are available to order for a fee.
