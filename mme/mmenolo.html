The department lends extensively to exhibitions in the UK and abroad. Many objects are also lent on a long-term basis, often for years at a time, to museums and galleries all over the UK. At present we have objects exhibited in around 30 other venues, whether museums, royal palaces or National Trust properties.A selection is given below.
