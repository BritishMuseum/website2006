7 June marks the 250th anniversary of the foundation of The British Museum in 1753 as the first public and national museum in the world. Enjoy this special day at the Museum by celebrating global cultures through dance, stories, music, myths and legends from around the world.
  • Vikings on the forecourt
  • Royal Philharmonic Orchestra
  • Celebrity readings
  • Stories of the world
  • World music in the galleries
  • Dance/performances
  • Candyfloss, ice cream, champagne or Pimms on the forecourt
  • Blue Peter presenter Konnie Huq in the Round Reading Room at 15.00
  • The winning entries of the Blue Peter Poster competition in the Great Court

All events are free!
