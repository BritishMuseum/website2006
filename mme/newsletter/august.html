Object of the Month - Pankratiasts, boxers and other athletes

Greek, about 500-475 BC
Made in Athens, Greece;
found at Vulci (today's Lazio, Italy)


Click image for full view

The pankration illustrated here was an Olympic sport and a type of all-in wrestling in which practically anything, including kicking or trying to strangle your opponent, was allowed. To find out more about the ancient competitors come and visit Olympia re-told in the Great Court.
