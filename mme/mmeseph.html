How to order

Orders for photographs of objects in the prehistoric and Romano-British collections can be placed in the following ways:

email: prehistoryandeurope@thebritishmuseum.ac.uk
phone: +44 (0)20 7323 8454
fax: +44 (0)20 7323 8588
post:

Department of Prehistory & Europe
Prehistory and Roman Britain
The British Museum
London WC1B 3DG


Orders for photographs of the medieval, Renaissance and modern collections (with the exception of the horological collection) can be placed with the photographic librarian, Ms. Sovati Smith, in the following ways:

email: ssmith@thebritishmuseum.ac.uk
phone: +44 (0)20 7323 8626
fax: +44 (0)20 7323 8496
post Department of Prehistory & Europe
Medieval and Modern Europe
The British Museum
London WC1B 3DG

Please note that requests for photographs from the horological collections should be directed to the Horological Study Room.

Please also note that it takes approximately four weeks to process an order.
