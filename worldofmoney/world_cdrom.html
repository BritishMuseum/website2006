World of Money - CD-ROM

Drawing on the collections of the British Museum, the World of Money CD-ROM presents a mine of information about the use, form, history and importance of money around the globe.

Packed with fun and information for all the family, World of Money features:

Direct access to dedicated World of Money website
2,000 images from the unrivalled collections of the British Museum
5 interactive games for repeat play with variable levels of difficulty
Clip art gallery for export to other applications
Video footage, animations, music and audio
Bookmark, print and save facilities

Interactive games to test your skill and knowledge
Treasure ship
Money Gallery
Money Maker
Pig Fortunes
Budget Power

A valuable resource for a wide range of topics at KS2 and KS3 in history, art, geography, IT, technology and for cross-curricular activities.
