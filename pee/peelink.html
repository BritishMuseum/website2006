  A new website, Virtually the Ice Age - www.creswell-crags.org.uk/ has been launched jointly by the British Museum, Creswell Heritage Trust and Derby Museum to explore the archaeology and palaeontology at Creswell Crags, Derbyshire. The website provides views of life and the changing environment during the Stone Age, together with the history of exploration of the site and a detailed catalogue of its finds.

The British & Irish Archaeological Bibliography (BIAB) http://www.britarch.ac.uk/biab/ provides the comprehensive guide to recent archaeological publications. Published twice-yearly, each issue contains hundreds of references, with abstracts, from a wide variety of journals and books
