The department has five Study Rooms which are available for research on the collections, open on weekdays by prior arrangement, 10.00 am - 1 pm and 2 pm - 4.30 pm.

Visitors are requested to make appointments in writing well in advance, provide as much detail as possible about the objects required for study, give an indication of the length of their visit, and include their contact details.

The study rooms also offer opinions on objects submitted by the public. This service is available without prior appointment on weekdays, 2 pm - 4:30 pm. Please note that the study rooms get very busy in the afternoons and that staff will only be able to advise on a limited number of objects at any given time.

View Study Room Regulations.
