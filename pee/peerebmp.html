  The Department has a strong reputation for publishing books, catalogues and articles covering many different aspects of Prehistoric and Romano-British Archaeology. Since 1996 staff from this Department have published 13 books and monographs, 2 edited volumes and 8 general books. In the same time staff have published over 120 papers in academic journals and more popular magazines.
