"..the Sainsbury galleries are a stunning success" Daily Telegraph

The Sainsbury African Galleries provides a substantial and permanent exhibition space for one of the finest collections of African art and artefacts in the world.

The collections comprises over 200,000 objects and encompasses both archeological and contemporary material, which includes both unique masterpieces of artistry and objects of everyday life. Highlights of the collection include a magnificent brass head of a Yoruba ruler from Ife, Nigeria; Asante goldwork from Ghana and the Torday collection of Central African sculpture, textiles and weaponry.

 The opening of the galleries marked the return of the magnificent African collections after nearly 30 years at the Museum of Mankind.
