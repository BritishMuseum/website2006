Personal Adornment

A striking array of jewellery and other accessories made from diverse materials are worn by both men and women in Africa. In addition, tattooing, scarification and body painting, elaborate hairstyles or hair shaving, the insertion of lip plates or ear plugs and exaggerating the shape of the head or neck are all means of adorning and transforming the body.

An impressive array of objects on display includes a lip plate from Sudan, beaded boots and a beaded crown from Nigeria, hinged silver anklets and an elaborately decorated lyre from Sudan which is elaborately decorated with strings of glass beads, cowrie shells, coins, rosaries, amulets and miscellaneous metal objects.
