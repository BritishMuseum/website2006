Japanese Fan Day
A free drop-in event for families

When: Saturday 2 August 2003, 1pm - 4pm We recommend arriving at 1pm, 2pm or 3pm and allowing up to one hour.

Where: The Japanese Galleries, Level 5, (Entrance at level 4), Montague Place Entrance

Come and make a fan for summer! We will have a special display of fan paintings to give you ideas for your own designs.The event will be run by Japanese artist, Mitsuko Hoshino-Pulverer, with Mavis Pilbeam. All materials and equipment are provided.

For children aged 3+; maximum 2 children per accompanying adult. Please remember: children must be accompanied by an adult at all times.

Every effort is made to ensure that galleries, events and facilities are accessible. For further information and access enquiries, please contact

Mavis Pilbeam, Department of Asia, Japanese Section,
(020)7323 8832 mpilbeam@thebritishmuseum.ac.uk
