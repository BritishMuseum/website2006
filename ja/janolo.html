Exhibitions loaned to Japan
The Department of Japanese Antiquities has loaned to a total of 24 exhibitions in Japan since 1990. Other departments, notably Oriental Antiquities and Prints and Drawings, also send regular loans. Complete exhibitions which have been sent by the British Museum are listed below. Starred exhibitions * were organised in conjunction with Asahi Shimbun.
