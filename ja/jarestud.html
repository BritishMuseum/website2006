Our Study Room is open, by appointment only, to members of the public from 10am - 4pm, Monday to Friday (excluding lunch break). We usually ask visitors to enter via the North Entrance on Montague Place and come to the lobby of the Department of Prints and Drawings on the Level 4. The member of staff on duty there will then phone through to the Japanese Department on your behalf.

Application to study the Japanese collections should be made in writing to the Department, stating your name, address and area of special interest specifying as clearly as possible the objects you would like to see. You must also include the name, address, and phone number of a referee, preferably academic, who can attest to your trustworthiness and confirm your interest. Applicants must be over the age of eighteen, although occasionally special arrangements may be made at the discretion of the Department. The Department will contact the referee, and if approved, will arrange an appointment with the applicant. Access to the collections is by appointment only and subject to availability.
