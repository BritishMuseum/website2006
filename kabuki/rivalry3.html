Indeed, Shikan’s career coincided with the golden age of Osaka print production, continuing in the end seventeen years longer than Rikan’s. He also performed regularly in Edo (Tokyo), a city whose acting style was quite different to the Osaka tradition that Rikan celebrated.To Rikan's mind, this made Shikan a slave to fame.
