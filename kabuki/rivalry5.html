After Shikan’s return from Edo in 1819, there was a surge of 'dream team' (mitate) prints and books which imagined the pair performing together as rivals. At the behest of their fans, Rikan and Shikan finally agreed in the summer of 1821 to perform together later that same year, but Rikan died in the ninth month before this dream could be realised. With Great Rikan no longer his rival, Shikan was left at the pinnacle of the acting world. Ever the showman, he continued to perform until his death in 1838 at the age of sixty.
