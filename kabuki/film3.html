Floating Weeds
Thursday 1 September, 18.30

'Gorgeous...this is a delight: a delicately coloured, beautifully acted melodrama that’s as measured as it is powerful'
METRO

Set in a small seaside town, Ozu’s elegantly simple plot tells of an ageing actor who returns home from touring. But former ties soon turn reunion into heartbreak.

Director: Yasujirō Ozu, Japan 1959, 119 mins
Stevenson Lecture Theatre, £3, concessions £2
