Through our partnerships with other museums we are developing a shared programme of new and innovative exhibitions. Also, we plan to collaborate with a network of local museums assisting with the development of their current and new displays.

We participate in the National Touring Exhibitions Programme (NTE) organised by the Hayward Gallery for the Arts Council of England. The NTE programme brings around 30 exhibitions, including some created exclusively by The British Museum, to approximately 1.4 million people in Britain annually.
