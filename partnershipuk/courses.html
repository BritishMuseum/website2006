more courses coming soon

The British Museum is committed to working in partnership with other museums and galleries throughout the United Kingdom. An important element of this partnership is to provide opportunities to share with other museum professionals the experiences and insight we have gained through our work.

Building on earlier initiatives, we are now offering a range of new courses developed in conjunction with regional museums. The courses are designed for those working in museums, galleries or heritage sites and are open to volunteers as well as paid staff. They will take
place at both The British Museum and regional venues, keeping cost to a minimum.

We are hoping to expand this programme in future years and would
like to receive ideas for further collaborations.

Rowena Loverance
Head of Outreach and Learning Resources
touringandpartnership@thebritishmuseum.ac.uk
