Panel made up of coloured glazed bricks

This panel is made up of polychrome glazed bricks and shows a guard. The guard is dressed in richly decorated Persian costume with a bow, quiver and spear and would have originally been part of a procession of similar guards. The bricks are made of ‘faience’ in brown, yellow, white, green and black.

This panel was reconstructed in 1999-2000.
