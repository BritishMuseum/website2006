OPINIONS ABOUT YOUR OBJECTS

This service is currently unavailable but will resume when the Asia Study Room reopens in January 2007. The below information will be applicable when the study room reopens.

Wednesdays only, from 10.00-13.00 and 14.15-16.00.

(a) The Asia Students' Room is open for the identification of objects Wednesdays only, between 10:00 - 13:00 and 14:15 - 16:00. This service does not require a Students' Room Ticket, but it is advisable to make an appointment.

Consultations with Department of Asia staff at any other times should be made by appointment only.

Objects may not be left on deposit.

(b) Objects for examination need to be accompanied by a pass issued at the Front Hall Information Desk. Neither the Trustees of the British Museum nor members of staff can accept liability for opinions given on objects submitted for examination, or for information provided. British Museum Staff are not permitted to provide either certificates of authenticity or valuations for objects.
