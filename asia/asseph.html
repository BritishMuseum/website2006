In February 2006 the Museum launched a new online picture library at www.bmimages.com. This website enables picture researchers, buyers and members of the general public, to search for images of objects in the Museum's collections and to buy high resolution digital images online using a credit card.

Customers who wish to buy digital images are advised first of all to look at www.bmimages.com to see if the image is available on the website – if so they can complete the purchase and download the image via the web using a credit card as a payment method.

If the image is not available on the website but you know it is of an object that is in the Department of Asia collection then please email us at asia@thebritishmuseum.ac.uk or contact us at one of the following;
