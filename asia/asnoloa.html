The Department actively participates in and lends material to a number of national and international exhibitions each year.

Loan out to
The British Empire and Commonwealth Museum, Bristol
26th September 2002 - 25th September 2007
A long term loan of Chinese ceramics and material from Ethnography to the Bristol Empire and Commonwealth Museum provides the opportunity to display some of our fine Chinese porcelains from the study collections in a new context exploring Britain's imperial role. Items on loan include this Chinese porcelain cup and saucer depicting the Crucifixion in famille rose enamels, Qing dynasty, c.1740-60, Franks collection, OA F.923+A. The design is one of a series showing the life of Christ, probably copied from a European illustrated bible.

Loan of 48 objects in total {27 from Ethnography, and 21 from Oriental}
