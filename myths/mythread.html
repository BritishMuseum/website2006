Further reading

 

Please note this is only a very selected reading list.

Greek myths - general
K. Dowden, The Uses of Greek Mythology (London: Routledge, 1994)
T. Gantz, Early Greek Myth: A Guide to Literary and Artistic sources (Baltimore: The Johns Hopkins University Press, 1996)
F. Graf, Greek Mythology: An Introduction (Baltimore: The Johns Hopkins University Press, 1996)
J. Morford and J.R. Lenardon, Classical Mythology (Oxford: Oxford University Press, 2000)
P. Grimal, The Penguin Dictionary of Classical Mythology (London: Penguin Books, 1991)

Greek myths in art
L. Burn, Greek Myths (London: British Museum Press, 1999)
T.H. Carpenter, Art and Myth in Ancient Greece (London: Thames and Hudson, 1991)
K. Schefold, Gods and Heroes in Late Archaic Greek Art (Cambridge: Cambridge University Press, 1992)
A. Shapiro, Myth into Art: Poet and Painter in Classical Greece (London: Routledge, 1994)
A. Snodgrass, Homer and the Artists: Text and Picture in Early Greek Art (Cambridge: Cambridge University Press, 1998)
S. Woodford, Images of Myth in Classical Antiquity (Cambridge: Cambridge University Press, 2003)

Greek myth - specific themes
M. J. Anderson, The Fall of Troy in Early Greek Poetry and Art (Oxford: Oxford University Press, 1997)
S. Woodford, The Trojan War in Ancient Art (London: Duckworth, 1993)
L.E. Doherty, Gender and the Interpretation of Classical Myth (London: Duckworth, 2001) M.R. Lefkowitz, Women in Greek Myth (Bristol: Bristol Classical Press, 1995)

Greek myths and their afterlife
I. Aghioni, C. Barbillon, and F. Lissarrague, Gods and Heroes of Classical Antiquity (Paris: Flammarion, 1996)
J. Hall, Dictionary of Subjects and Symbols in Art (London: John Murray, 1974)
M.R. Scherer, The Legends of Troy in Art and Literature (New Yort and London: Phaidon Press, 1964)
