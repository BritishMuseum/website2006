Current Opportunities

As one of London’s leading tourist attractions and academic institutions, the British Museum is home to a vast collection, numbering approximately six million objects. From gallery assistants to curators, janitors to administrators, whether they are managing the collections, visitors or resources, each member of the British Museum’s 900 strong workforce helps to ensure that each of our 5 million annual visitors has a memorable visit and will come back again and again. All of our current vacancies are advertised here.
