Queen of the Night

Since 1991 we have participated in the National Touring Exhibitions Programme (NTE) organised by the Hayward Gallery for the Arts Council of England. The NTE programme brings around 30 exhibitions, including some created exclusively by The British Museum, to approximately 1.4 million people in Britain annually.
