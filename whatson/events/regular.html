Reading Room

The historic Reading Room is now home to the Walter and Leonore Annenberg Centre. Open to Museum visitors from 10.00 every day, it contains the Paul Hamlyn Library and COMPASS, an interactive multimedia database of the collections. Come in and find out more about the objects and cultures represented in the British Museum.

For more information
Telephone: +44 (0)20 7323 8838

Hands On

Joined trained volunteers to get Hands On with a range of unique Museum objects from prehistory to the present day. Touch ancient Greek pottery and coins, try on a Roman ring, grapple with an astrolabe or investigate unusual forms of money. Suitable for children and adults. Facilitated by The British Museum Friends.
Monday to Friday, 11.00 to 16.00 Enlightenment Gallery
   
Daily, 11.00 to 16.00 Room 68: British Currencies, Fakes and Forgeries, Art Medals, What is Money? >> more about Coins & Medals
   
Tuesday to Thursday, 11.00 to 16.00 Room 72: AG Leventis Gallery of Cypriot Antiquities
   
Tuesday to Thursday 11.00 to 16.00 Room 33: Hotung Gallery: China
