Syria study day
Saturday 9 December, 10.00 - 17.00
BP Lecture Theatre
£20, Members £18, concessions £15, 300 places

Syria became one of the major crossroads of civilisations – from the Egyptians and Hittites to the Crusaders and Ottomans. Join our experts for a fascinating introduction to the culture and heritage of Syria, from ancient to modern times.

Turquoise mosaics from Mexico
Saturday 9 December, 10.00 - 13.30
 Stevenson Lecture Theatre
£15, Members and concessions £12

This study session will provide a succinct and fascinating introduction to the magnificent Aztec/Mixtec turquoise mosaics in the British Museum, featuring important new findings and illustrated by stunning images.

Exploring drawing
10 Monday afternoons from 9 October
 Studio (CEC)
£60 (no concessions)

See the British Museum through an artist's eyes. Drawing from the sculpture and objects on display, with guidance on technique, media and style, you will gain an insight into our cultural heritage as well as enhancing your drawing skill. Held jointly with the Workers Education Association.
